Hailey Bieber Is the Coolest It Girl in a Navy Blazer and Matching Cargo Pants

May 01, 2024
blog

Hailey Bieber is maintaining her trendsetter status through the seasons.

As we enter fall and the cozy dressing that comes with it, the model is giving us a lesson in pairing unexpected staples.

Yesterday, Bieber stepped out in West Hollywood to attend a Lacoste event in a cool and sophisticated streetwear look. The Rhode founder styled an oversized navy blazer with a pair of loose-fitting cargo pants in the same hue and a matching bandeau top. For shoes, she opted for black open-toe heels, and she carried a green leather handbag.

The all-navy look was on-brand with Lacoste, known for its blue-and-white and green-and-white designs.

BACKGRID

Bieber's classy style is a constant inspiration for her fans. Over the past couple of years, she has put her own spin on Princess Diana's iconic blazer-and-bike-shorts look, as well as perfected the chunky-sneakers-and-socks trend. She is constantly finding new ways to style menswear to make it feel sexy and playing with oversized pieces, such as jackets, blazers, and sweatshirts.

Recently, Bieber even debuted her own collection in collaboration with WARDROBE.NYC. The capsule collection includes minimalist, luxury basics that mirror her style and are meant to be worn over and over again.

More than that, Bieber has created her own "clean girl" aesthetic, which essentially means less is usually more. The model applies the motto to her fashion, as well as to her enviable "glazed donut" skin care routine.
