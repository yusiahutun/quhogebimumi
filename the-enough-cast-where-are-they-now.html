The Enough Cast: Where Are They Now?

May 27, 2024
blog

The “Enough” cast and crew consists of over ten actors and actresses who worked on the movie in 2002. “Enough” is an American thriller film based on the 1998 novel “Black and Blue” by Anna Quindlen. The movie premiered in the United States over two decades ago and received negative reviews from film critics.

Despite the negative reviews and comments, the film recorded success and grossed nearly $52 million. Since its release, “Enough” has been subject to a critical re-evaluation of its cast’s performance. However, it has been dubbed an underrated thriller by many, which has made fans curious about the “Enough” cast. 

Some have experienced significant career breakthroughs since the film premiered and are now prominent in Hollywood. However, others haven’t. Here’s a glimpse into the life of some of the “Enough” cast and where they are now.

Jennifer Lopez (Slim Hiller)

Jennifer Lopez was not a mainstream actress before starring as Slim Hiller in the controversial 2002 film. In the thriller, she played the role of an abused wife seeking revenge on a cheating and abusive husband.

After the movie premiered, she earned a nomination for a Golden Raspberry Award for Worst Actress. However, it didn’t faze her. Despite the backlash and negative reviews, the stunning actress is a multi-award-winning star today. 

Since she appeared as one of the “Enough” cast over two decades ago, she has sold over 80 million records worldwide. In addition to being a successful singer, she has appeared in several movies. Some of the films she has appeared in include “Hustlers,” “Marry Me,” and her most recent work, “The Mother,” which is making waves as an action movie.

Lopez has a net worth of $400,000,000. Hence, she is one of the most successful female entertainers in the world and lives a life of luxury with her husband, Ben Affleck, and children.  

Billy Campbell (Mitch Hiller)

Billy Campbell is another “Enough” cast whose role propelled him to fame and success. Campbell’s role as Mitch Hiller, the abusive husband of Slim Hiller, enhanced his career and landed him several projects. After the film premiered, Campbell played the serial killer Ted Bundy in the 2003 movie, “The Stranger Beside Me.”

Afterward, the talented actor starred in the Canadian TV show “Cardinal.” He won the Canadian Screen Award for Best Actor in a Limited Series or TV Film at the 6th, 7th, and 8th Canadian Screen Awards in 2018, 2019, and 2020 respectively. The multi-award-winning actor has a net worth of $2,500,000 and lives in Norway with his wife, Anna Campbell, and their children.

Tessa Allen (Gracie Hiller)

Allen’s role as Mitch and Slim’s daughter, Gracie Hiller, was intriguing. The then-young actress made her acting debut as an “Enough” cast. Following her appearance in the film, the stunning actress landed recurring roles in the TV shows “Providence” and “General Hospital.” In addition, she also guest-starred on an episode of “CSI: Miami.”

However, her promising career as an actress ended after she appeared in the 2012 Indie drama “Arcadia.” Unlike her on-screen parents, the budding star has not appeared in any recent production. Nevertheless, the stunning actress graduated with a major in Neuroscience in 2019. According to her LinkedIn, she is a graduate student at the University of Colorado Anschutz Medical Campus.

Juliette Lewis (Ginny, Slim’s Friend)

Lewis is another “Enough” cast member that has taken the entertainment industry by storm since the film premiered. Although she played Ginny, a supporting role as the friend of Slim, she has landed prominent roles in other movies. Since the film, Lewis has graced the screen with her captivating performance in movies like “Cold Creek Manor” (2003), “The Switch” (2010), and “Hellion” (2014).

Aside from these, Lewis also appeared in the Showtime thriller series, “Yellowjackets,” in 2022. The stunning actress has also earned nominations for an Academy Award, a Golden Globe, and a Primetime Emmy Award. Lewis has a net worth of $5,000,000, and like most “Enough” cast members, she is living her best life. 

Dan Futterman (Joe, Slim’s Former Boyfriend)

Futterman played Joe, Slim’s ex-boyfriend, in the film. However, he has gone on to play several roles in other projects. While Futterman has not acted in any recent projects, he has worked behind the cameras as a screenwriter and producer.

Following his appearance in “Enough,” some notable projects the multitalented actor has appeared in are “A Mighty Heart” (2007) and “Political Animals” (2012). Although his exact net worth is unknown, Futterman is married to TV writer and producer Anya Epstein, and they live a life of luxury with their children. 

Janet Carroll (Mrs. Hiller, Mitch’s Mom)

In contrast to some of the “Enough” cast members, Carroll already had a solid entertainment career before she appeared in the “Enough” movie. She had a recurring role as Peggy LaRue Nelson in the 1983 TV show “Hill Street Blues.”

Following her role as Mitch Hiller’s mom in “Enough,” she starred in the Broadway original musical “Little Women” as Aunt March. Unfortunately, Caroll died on May 22, 2012, at 71, from complications related to brain cancer at her home in Manhattan.
