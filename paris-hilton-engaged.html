Paris Hilton Engaged - Paris Hilton Engaged to Chris Zylka

April 30, 2024
blog

Paris Hilton and actor Chris Zylka are officially engaged. The hotel heiress made the announcement this morning on Instagram whens he posted a few snaps from their snowy proposal. Zylka got down on one knee in Aspen, Colorado over the weekend, after the two had lunch during their ski trip, People reports.

"I feel like the luckiest girl in the world! You are my dream come true! Thank you for showing me that fairytales do exist," Hilton captioned her post.

Paris apparently didn't see the proposal coming. "I was so excited and surprised!" she told People. "I immediately said yes!"

One of the photos in her slideshow includes a glimpse of her massive ring, which features a pear-shaped rock half the size of her finger. "The ring was so gorgeous and sparkling. I was shaking as I put it on. It is the most beautiful ring that I have ever seen!" Hilton added.

Hilton, 36, and Zylka, 32, first met at an Oscar party eight years ago, People reports, but only reconnected two years ago. She'd only made their relationship Instagram-official in February 2017, when she posted a photo with her now-fiancé and actress Ashley Benson.
