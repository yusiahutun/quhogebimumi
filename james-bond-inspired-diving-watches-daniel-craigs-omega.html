James Bond-inspired diving watches Daniel Craigs Omega Seamaster from No Time to Die and 5 mo

August 15, 2024
blog

James Bond’s relationships with women do not last long, but his relationship with Omega is another story. As Omega is one of 007’s favourite diver watches, and as the anticipated latest instalment No Time to Die will mark the 25th anniversary with the luxury watchmaker, we are presenting our top six high-end diving watches, from Omega to Ulysse Nardin.

They combine sporty-sophisticated designs with avant-garde material which is perfect to wear when you are under the sea, close to the shore or playing the perfect spy.

5 of Daniel Craig’s best movies that aren’t James Bond films

Omega 

Famously worn by our favourite spy Bond himself, the Seamaster Diver 300M is modernised in a 42mm model, integrating the Super-Luminova chronometer calibre for enhanced visibility. Crafted from stainless steel, its stunning dial is refined with black ceramic and laser-engraved waves. Price upon request.

Rolex

The Oyster Perpetual Sea-Dweller, equipped with calibre 3235 and waterproof to 1,220 metres, combines Oystersteel and 18-carat yellow gold for a sophisticated and bold look. Price on request.

These celebs are mad for motorsports watches – should you be, too?

Panerai

Highly water-resistant (up to 300 metres), the Luminor Marina Carbotech 44mm features a Carbotech case and a sandwich dial with blue Super-Luminova markings, HK$97,500 (US$12,549).

Which leading man wore the coolest watch to the Oscars?

Breitling

The Superocean Automatic 36 is water resistant to 200 metres, and with a sturdy stainless steel case, a white dial and a white rubber strap, blends versatility, performance and style. Ideal for active women. Price on request.

5 women’s watches for the month of love, fit for the red carpet

Tudor

The Pelagos watch has a 70-hour power reserve, has a titanium case, a helium escape valve and a luminescent display. It is waterproof to 500 metres. Price on request.

A. Lange & Söhne gets its first ever stainless steel collection

Ulysse Nardin

The Diver Deep Dive is water-resistant to 1,000 metres. Hammerhead sharks and luminescent hour markers adorn the blue dial. The watch boasts a self-winding calibre UN-320 and a screw-on decompression valve system. Price on request.
