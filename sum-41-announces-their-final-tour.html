Sum 41 Announces Their Final Tour

September 08, 2024
blog

TORONTO (CelebrityAccess) Canadian pop-punk/alt-rock band Sum 41 announced plans for their a farewell tour, with shows scheduled for North America, Europe, and Asia.

Sum 41’s ‘Tour Of The Setting Sum’ is billed as the band’s final headlining tour and kicks off with a run of Asian shows, starting on March 1st at Jakarta’s Uptown Park and wrapping at Punkspring in Osaka, Japan on March 23rd.

The North American leg of the tour starts at The Astro in Omaha, Nebraska on April 19th and closes out on May 19th at Chartway Arena in Norfolk, VA. Along the way, Sum 41 will play in markets such as Boston, Asbury Park, NJ, Nashville, and Baltimore.

In June 2024, Sum 41 heads to Europe, for a run of shows that starts on June 14th at Rock For People in the Czech Republic and concludes at Nos Alive in Lisbon, Portugal on July 13th.

After a brief run of Canadian shows in August, Sum 41 return to the U.S. in September and October, before playing their final show at Toronto’s Scotiabank Arena on January 30th, 2025.

The tour supports Sum 41’s final album Heaven :x: Hell, which is due at your favorite retailer or music streamer via Rise Records on March 29th.
