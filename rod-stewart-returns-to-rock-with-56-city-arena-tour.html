Rod Stewart Returns To Rock With 56-City Arena Tour

May 22, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Rod Stewart returns to his rock roots with the launch of his 56-city North American arena tour kicking off on January 12 at Fort Lauderdale's BankAtlantic Center. Touring in support of his latest covers album, "Still The Same…Great Rock Classics Of Our Time," the tour wraps up on May 9 in Albany, NY at Pepsi Arena.

Promoted by AEG Live, among Stewart’s concert stops will be New York, Chicago, Boston, Philadelphia, Detroit, Atlanta, Washington, DC, and Toronto. The tour will feature a rotating in-the-round stage; Stewart will be backed by a nine-piece band and three back-up singers. — Jane Cohen and Bob Grossweiner
