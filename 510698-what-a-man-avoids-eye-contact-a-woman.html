Eye contact is a great nonverbal tool that improves communication with another person. Normally, a man should be able to maintain eye contact with you as it makes him appear confident. Eye contact strengthens your relationship because you can tell what the other person thinks. So what does it mean when a man avoids eye contact with a woman?

It is disconcerting when a guy you are talking to, involved with, or interested in avoids making eye contact with you. Without knowing what it means, you get frustrated. You start coming up with different explanations or wondering what you are doing wrong.

What does it mean when a man avoids eye contact with a woman?

There are many reasons a man would avoid eye contact with you. This can range from attraction to guilt, social anxiety, or lack of interest. Below is a look at some of these reasons:

1. He is sad or angry about something

Emotional expressions change around the eyes, communicating what you feel to others. You avoid eye contact when you don't want someone to see what you're feeling (especially a negative emotion).

When a guy avoids eye contact, he could be hiding negative emotions so as not to worry you or for fear of appearing vulnerable. He could be sad or angry about something that has happened, or it could be that he is angry with you and doesn't want to confront you.

2. He is attracted to you

Does a guy avoid eye contact if he likes you? The answer is yes. There are several reasons why a guy might avoid eye contact if he is into you. First, he might be nervous to show that he likes you, especially if he is uncertain if you feel the same. In such a case, you can find that he breaks eye contact when you look at him.

Does avoiding eye contact mean attraction? It's also possible that a guy is attracted to you but is trying to play it cool, hence the lack of eye contact. Lastly, he could avoid eye contact deliberately to pique your interest and curiosity.

3. He is shy or introverted

Shyness is another top reason a man might avoid eye contact with you. Many shy people, especially introverts or those suffering from social anxiety, tend to avoid making eye contact. This can be due to feeling uncomfortable with new people or a new environment or as a means to avoid awkward moments when talking to others.

Another thing to note is that shy people might avoid eye contact whether they like you or not. So, the best thing to do with such a person is to start slowly and get to know them. Once they are comfortable with you, they might have an easier time making and maintaining eye contact.

4. He feels intimidated by you

Insecurity could be another explanation when a man avoids eye contact with a woman. In such a case, he might not be shy or suffer from social anxiety, but he could feel intimidated by you for one reason. For example, he might have low self-esteem, making him think you are out of his league.

The guy might also like you but could be worried that he will make a fool of himself by interacting with you. He could also be intimidated by your confidence and personality. The best you can do in such a situation is start a conversation with him and try to put him at ease.

5. He doesn’t want to interact with you

Sometimes, there is no hidden meaning when a guy avoids eye contact with you; it could simply be a sign that he is not interested in interacting with you. He could find your conversation boring and thus avoid eye contact as a way to end it. Or he could be uncomfortable with the environment you are in and want to leave.

6. He is not attracted to you

Sometimes, avoiding eye contact indicates that someone is not attracted to you. It's also possible that the guy is avoiding eye contact not to mislead you or is scared to tell you that he is not attracted to you. Either way, you cannot do anything about this situation.

7. He is involved with someone else

When a man avoids eye contact with a woman, it might mean he's involved with someone else. It could also be possible that he is not in a relationship but is interested in someone else.

8. He is hiding something from you

Lack of eye contact is a sign of deception. If a man is avoiding eye contact all of a sudden, he is likely hiding something from you. A man who is feeling guilty could avoid eye contact so as not to feel judged, accused, exposed, or vulnerable.

He could also be doing it so as not to get caught in a lie, thus leading to confrontation. It would help to look for other signs of deception besides eye contact. This includes fidgeting, a tense posture, saying too little or too much, and being defensive.
