    How to Choose the Best Makeup Brush:

    Choosing the best makeup brush requires you to pay attention to several factors. Here we have listed the most important ones so that you can make an informed choice:

    Purpose: 

    Not all makeup brushes are built the same. There are different types for different purposes. For instance, there's a different brush for applying foundation, typically dense and dome-like. A lip liner brush is thinner and pointy to help with precise application. Therefore, you must determine exactly why you need a makeup brush to narrow down your choices.

    Brush Shape:

    Because different makeup brushes serve different makeup brushes, they come in several different shapes. They can be flat, tapered, angled, rounded, thin, stippling, or fan-like. While fluffy or rounded brushes are good for powder application and blending, a fan brush is ideal for applying highlighter.

    Bristle Material:

    Bristles in makeup brushes are made from both synthetic and natural materials. While natural bristles made from animal hair and fur may feel super soft, they don't always find favor with every makeup enthusiast, especially if they have vegan or cruelty-free concerns. Synthetic brushes, on the other hand, are more cost-effective, easy to maintain, and resistant to bacterial growth as they are made from nylon or Taklon. Choose according to your preferences.

    Size And Density:

    The size of the brush and the density of the bristles decide how much coverage you will receive. More bristles mean more coverage. They are considered ideal for applying and blending foundation, whereas makeup brushes with fewer bristles are more suitable for applying setting powder.

    Handle Length:

    The length of the handle is a matter of personal preference. Some people are more comfortable with shorter handles as they offer more control, while others prefer longer handles for their comfortable grip. You must also choose as per your preferences and convenience. 

    Reputed Brands:

    It is best to purchase a makeup brush from reputed brands and retailers. This ensures that the brushes are authentic and high-quality. They are softer, perform better, and can last longer than cheap brushes.

    Budget:

    Makeup brushes are available at various price points. You must ascertain how much money you are willing to spend on a makeup brush. However, before deciding you must acquaint yourself with the quality of makeup brushes available at different prices. Investing is brush sets or combo packs is a great way to save some bucks!

    How to Use a Makeup Brush:

    It's just a makeup brush. How hard could it be to use it, right? Well, there's a difference between using a makeup brush and using it correctly. So, here's a step-by-step guide on how to use a makeup brush for the best results:

    Step 1: Start with a clean and moisturized face. Do your skincare routine as you usually would, and don't forget to wear sunscreen.

    Step 2: Once you have given your skin its daily dose of moisturization, it's time to get to makeup.

    Step 3: For this, you must pick out the appropriate brushes depending on the type of makeup you intend to do. For instance, pick an angled brush to apply eyeliner, a flat or a stripple brush to apply foundation or a fluffy round brush for blending.

    Step 4: Dip the relevant brush into the product and apply it evenly. For foundation, start at the center of your face and move outwards with gentle strokes. For concealer, you must take a concealer brush and use it to dab the product over the areas and imperfections you wish to conceal.

    Step 5: Smile wide and you will see the apple of your cheeks. Take a blush brush and sweep it gently in an upward motion. 

    Step 6: For eyeshadow, use a flat brush to get the base color. You may use a smaller brush to add details. An angled brush is best for applying eyeliner and defining the brows.    

    Step 7: For blending, use a fluffy brush to achieve a seamless finish.

    Try out different makeup brushes and see what works best for your needs and preferences. Practice with the brushes to perfect your application and blending. And don’t forget to clean your brushes regularly.

    FAQ's

    Are expensive makeup brushes better?

    Yes, expensive makeup brushes are better as they come with a guarantee of quality and durability.

    How do I know if my makeup brushes are good quality?

    The best indicator of a good-quality makeup brush is its softness. It must feel soft and gentle when used against the skin. Also, it’s bristles will remain intact and won’t fall out.

    How often should I wash my makeup brushes?

    You must wash your makeup brushes every 5 to 10 days.

    Can two people use the same brush?

    No, two people cannot use the same brush. If you share a brush, you also end up sharing the impurities, bacteria, and infections on each other’s skin. This transfer of pathogens is not good for your skin.
