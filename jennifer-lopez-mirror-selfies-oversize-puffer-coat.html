Jennifer Lopez Looks Like She Could Take a Nap Under This Oversize Puffer Coat

May 05, 2024
blog

In Jennifer Lopez’s latest Instagram post, she’s wearing a puffer so fluffy and so large, it looks like she could toss it on her bed and use it as a makeshift comforter. (Maybe she was inspired after seeing Beyoncé’s bedlike silver puffer on the Renaissance World Tour.)

J.Lo showed off the statement outerwear in a series of selfies that seem to have been taken in Japan, if her caption is any indication. It read: “Konnichiwa 💚♾️.”

The slideshow sees the singer and actor in a gray knit turtleneck sweater under an oversize porcelain-colored puffer coat, with large lapels standing up alongside Lopez’s head. In the final slide, we get a small glimpse at the gray sweatpants the Hustlers star paired with the sweater. She accessorized with ginormous mirror-frame sunglasses. (Clearly the theme of the outfit was “Go big or go home.”)

Beauty-wise, Jenny From the Block opted for a rosy lip, and had her hair pulled back into a tight bun. As she was holding her phone to take the selfies, she also gave us a look at her sharp, camel-painted nails—oh, and her hummingbird-covered phone case.

This phone case is likely a nod to Lopez’s hubby, Ben Affleck. For her just-released ninth studio album, This Is Me…Now, she wrote a song inspired by their romance and called it “Hummingbird.” Hummingbirds also play a notable narrative role in the musical film she released alongside the album, This Is Me…Now: A Love Story.

J.Lo talked about writing the song back in 2022, saying, “To me, hummingbirds are messengers of love. They’re also the fastest bird, but they always have time to stop, eat something sweet, and smell the roses. I identify with them, but more than anything, whenever I see one, I feel like it’s a sign from God that everything is going to be okay.”
