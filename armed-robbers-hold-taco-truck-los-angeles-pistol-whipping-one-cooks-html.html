CCTV captures armed robbery suspects holding up LA taco truck

February 09, 2024
blog

Three men have been caught on camera robbing a taco truck in Los Angeles.

The armed bandits were videoed on CCTV and seen pistol-whipping at least one employee when the robbery occurred last month. 

CCTV shows the three suspects, described as two black men and one Hispanic, all aged in their mid-20s, running on to the truck while brandishing a pistol. 

The robbery took place at the Tacos El Gallazo truck in South Los Angeles on July 14.

Police are hunting for two black men and one Hispanic man, all aged in their mid-20s

The a brutal armed robbery on the taco truck in Los Angeles took place last month

Two workers were held at gunpoint during the incident according toCBS Los Angeles.

Despite the brazen attack, the suspects failed in their goal to open a safe that was located on board the truck and instead, they were forced to demand cell phones and cash from the workers.

Video captures one suspect pistol-whipping a worker before the group then fled on foot.

One of the workers at the Tacos El Gallazo truck was pistol-whipped during the attack

The robbers attempted to steal from the truck's safe but took the workers' phones and cash after being unable to get insides 

One worker, who is in the kitchen area at the time, is forced to his knees before the other worker is brought inside and told to kneel as well.

The man who was struck ended up needing stitches, but was back at work last week.

The men could then be seen handing over their phones and wallets before one of the suspects, dressed in a black hooded sweater, hit a worker in the side of the head with the butt of his pistol. 

CCTV on board the truck captured the men's faces in crystal clear detail

The attack was especially violent and not like others that had occurred in the area recently

The police have not released the names of the suspects but have described two as black and one as Hispanic. All are believed to be in their mid-20s.

Robbers then began to search the two workers before one of them pulled another phone from one of their pockets. The robbers also looked through the trucks cupboards.  

Workers at another truck in the same area of south Los Angeles say they were also robbed, but described this latest attack as 'very serious' 

Police urge anyone with information about the robbery to call detectives at 323-786-5422.
