Emilio Estevez's biography

The actor was born on 12th May 1962 (61 yrs as of 2023) in New York City, U.S. He is the oldest child of famous actor Martin Sheen (Ramon Estevez-father) and artist Janet Sheen (mother). His father is a devout Catholic, while his mother is a strict Southern Baptist.

Emilio Estevez's paternal grandparents were Irish and Spanish descendants. He has two brothers, Ramon Estevez and Charlie Sheen (born Carlos Estévez), and a sister Renée Estevez.

The actor started schooling in the New York City public school system but later transferred to a private academy once his father's career took off. He grew up on Manhattan's Upper West Side until his family moved west in 1968 (when his father was featured in Catch-22).

Career

Estevez started his acting career in the 1980s as a member of the acting troupe the Brat Pack. His first role was in a drama produced by Catholic Paulist Order. He then made his stage debut in Mister Roberts, which he played together with his father, Ramon Sheen.
