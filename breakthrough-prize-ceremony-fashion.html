The 2024 Breakthrough Prize Ceremony: The 10 best dressed

August 20, 2024
blog

Getty Images

Margot Robbie in Rick Owens

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

With the bright pink Barbie tour fully in her rear view mirror, Margot Robbie has been embracing a wardrobe of slick black evening dresses lately. For the Breakthrough Prize, she wore one of our favourites yet, a strapless black design from Rick Owens, which she teamed with chunky gold bangles.

Katy Perry in Coperni

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Katy Perry was another star to make a case for the black gown. She wore a structured design from Coperni's latest collection, which she teamed with the label's viral Air Swipe bag.

Lizzo in Quine Li

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

The beautiful black gowns continued with Lizzo's custom Quine Li design, which featured an exaggerated shoulder and train. She teamed it with a sharp bob and pointy pumps.

Behati Prinsloo in vintage Jean Paul Gaultier

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Behati Prinsloo dipped into the archives for her look. The model chose a design from Jean Paul Gaultier's spring/summer 1995 collection, a strapless pinstripe dress.

Michelle Yeoh in Tom Ford

Taylor Hill//Getty Images

Keeping things super classic was Michelle Yeoh, who wore a double-breasted white suit by Tom Ford with silver accessories, including a sparkly Balenciaga bag.

Olivia Wilde in Galvan

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Olivia Wilde wore a sleeved black dress by Galvan, which featured a huge cut-out detail up one side and a dramatic white bow.

Zoe Saldana in Givenchy

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Zoe Saldana embraced a little sparkle for the evening. The actress wore a strapless black gown by Givenchy which was absolutely covered in sequins. She accessorised with silver jewellery and a red lip.

Brie Larson in Chanel

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Brie Larson marked the start of spring with a floral ballgown by Chanel, which had a halter neckline. She styled it with an updo and peeptoe heels.

Venus Williams in Prada

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Venus Williams was one of the few stars on the night to embrace some serious colour. The sports star wore a bright pink, floor-length gown with silver straps by Prada.

Jessica Chastain in Elie Saab

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Jessica Chastain always bring a touch of glamour to proceedings. She wore a purple sequinned dress by Elie Saab with a low V neckline and pockets.
