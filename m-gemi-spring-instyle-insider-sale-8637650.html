Hearing the word “sale” is music to my ears, so when InStyle launched an exclusive InSider Sale, where you can access deals curated just for you from brands like Cariuma, Andie Swim, Gilt, and Ilia, I just about jumped for joy. But there’s one retailer I’m particularly excited for: M.Gemi.

Though, you can’t forget about Zoe Saldaña, who has worn my comfortable go-to Danza flat.  

8 M.Gemi InStyle Insider Sale Picks

M.Gemi Danza Flat

I own the shoe in black—while eight colors including a cherry red are up for grabs—and have worn it on my commutes to work, formal dinners, and while trekking through New York City. The foldable design even lets me stash the pair away in my purse, allowing it to become an emergency shoe for when I’m wearing heels.   

The luxe ballet slipper features a slim silhouette, spacious square toe, chic bow, and cushioned interior. Hidden between layers of buttery leather and a durable sole is a 20-millimeter wedge that adds support, boosts height, and sets it apart from competitors.

While M.Gemi won me over with its coveted Danza Flat, I’m snagging further footwear while it’s 20 percent off. So, keep scrolling to discover what else I’m adding to my virtual shopping bag. 

The Mafalda Sandal

The Mafalda Sandal is definitely coming home with me thanks to its elegant design, slightly raised heel, and slip on-and-off silhouette. Made of Italian leather and featuring delicate stitching, this is a sandal you’ll wear for years to come with straight-leg jeans and button-up shirts. Shop it in six designs, including gold metallic, denim and leopard print.  

The Palestra Due Sneaker

Everyone needs a staple white sneaker, and this Palestra Due tennis shoe is high on my list. It includes flexible leather, flat laces, a perforated footbed, and lightweight design. The minimal shoe requires “no break-in period,” according to the brand, and I’m eager to put that claim to the test. 

The Lisinda Mesh Kitten Heel

I recently made the decision to swap my soaring pumps for comfortable kitten heels, and I’m never turning back. These Lisinda slingbacks don’t only cater to my new lifestyle, but incorporate the trendy mesh design everyone from Jennifer Lawrence to Chrissy Teigen are wearing. Snag your choice pair in black, beige, and a sexy cherry red. 

The Matilde Due Sandal

If you’re on the hunt for another celeb-worn piece, then this is it. The Matilde Due Sandal is an elevated buckle slide styled by Gigi Hadid and editors alike. A contoured footbed, combination of textiles, and adjustable straps makes this sandal standout, while offering you a do-it-all shoe for the spring season. Dress it up with maxi dresses, or make it casual with cargo pants and a T-shirt. 

Shop more M.Gemi shoes for 20 percent off with the code INSTYLE20 until April 28, before the InStyle InSider Sale comes to an end. And remember, it’s a sitewide sale, so you might as well add handbags and men’s footwear to your cart, too!
