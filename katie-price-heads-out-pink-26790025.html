Katie Price heads out in pink tracksuit and slippers as she gets feet injections

March 20, 2024

Katie Price has stepped out in a pink tracksuit and slippers after updating fans about her recent health woes.

The OnlyFans star, 43, was pictured in the comfortable matching fleece ensemble while running errands in London on Sunday alongside her son Harvey, 19.

Mother-of-five Katie could be seen talking on her phone and appeared in relatively good spirits as she rested her injured feet in fluffy cream slippers.

She was also pictured with her engagement ring on display.

The outing followed Katie receiving cortisone injection treatment into her feet after recently revealing she was "struggling to walk" two years on from her horror holiday fall.

In July 2020, Katie smashed both of her feet after jumping off a wall at a waterpark during a holiday in Turkey.

Katie also recently gave fans an update about her condition.

"I've asked a lot of people about cortisol injections and it works for some people and it doesn’t for some others. But fingers crossed it does.

"If it doesn't work, the other option is to go back into my feet and look at the screws and see what can happen."
