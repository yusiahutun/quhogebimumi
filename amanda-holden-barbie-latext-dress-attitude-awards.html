The Britain's Got Talent judge was a candy pink princess in a secret look from the behind-the scenes at the Attitude Awards 2023

    Amanda Holden was the definition of a style chameleon on Wednesday night as she debuted not one, but four spellbinding outfits to co-host the Virgin Atlantic Attitude Awards 2023 with her close friend, Alan Carr. 

    Dancing onto the red carpet at London's iconic Camden Roundhouse, the Britain's Got Talent judge started her night in a billowing satin gown from Celia Kritharioti. 

    Complete with a fitted bodice, sweetheart neckline and an ultra-mini skirt adorned with a frothy train in a midnight black hue, Amanda's daring dress, styled by Karl Willet, was hailed as "breathtaking" by fans on Instagram. 

    Later, the Heart Radio star channelled her inner as she transformed herself into a plastic fantastic replica of the famous Mattel doll.

    Amanda's third look of the night was a dramatic feathered gown from David Koma.
