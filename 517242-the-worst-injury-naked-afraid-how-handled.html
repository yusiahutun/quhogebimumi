1. EJ Snyder

EJ Snyder almost lost his genitals after falling from a tree branch into a swamp. He sustained multiple lacerations to his scrotum. When the incident happened, EJ was given two options. The show producers gave him the option of quitting the show and going to the hospital for treatment.

EJ was also given the option of receiving immediate medical attention on-site and proceeding with the filming of the show. But, there were serious health risks if he opted to stay in the competition. The medics told the producers that he could suffer an infection.

In the worst-case scenario, EJ could lose his genitalia. The injury could be fatal if EJ became septic. Despite the health risks, EJ opted to continue filming the show. He received eight stitches on the wound.

The medics ordered him to stay out of water for ten days to allow the wound to heal properly. During this period, the medics also advised him to avoid overly exerting himself.

2. Sam Mouzer

Sam Mouzer is one of the contestants who suffered serious injuries during the filming of the show. He suffered a serious injury to his penis during season 15.

Mouzer and his partner were sleeping beside a fire when the incident happened. A spark shot out from the flames and sizzled his penis. The medics were able to treat the injury, and he continued filming the show.

Has anyone died on Naked and Afraid?

No one has died on Naked and Afraid. One of the contestants, Melanie Rauscher, died, but her death had nothing to do with the filming of the reality show. She died at her residence. An autopsy conducted on her body revealed that she died after she accidentally inhaled compressed air used in cleaning products.

Has anyone been attacked on Naked and Afraid?

Since the contestants are in the wild, the chances of being attacked by wild animals are high. For instance, Steve Ranking was bitten by a pit viper in 2013 when inspecting the show premises. He was immediately rushed to the hospital by helicopter before the venom spread.

In another incident, a contestant and producer were attacked by reptiles as they were filming the show. Contestants have described the pain of being attacked by mosquitoes as their worst nightmare. Manu Toigo contracted dengue fever after he was attacked by mosquitoes.

During season 3, Matt Wright got a bug bite on his foot. The medics treated the bite using antibiotics, and it did not develop an infection. Later, he discovered he had a flesh-eating bacteria on the injured site. The bacteria started spreading, and he risked losing his foot. He had no option but to tap out of that season.

Why don't they eat monkeys on Naked and Afraid?

Even though the contestants are in an extreme wilderness environment, they are not allowed to catch and eat monkeys or any other endangered animal. The monkeys are protected in many regions, and they may find themselves on the wrong side of the law when they kill them. Besides, they risk being attacked by monkeys when they kill one of them.
