Kirk Franklin, Yolanda Adams, Fred Hammond, Marvin Sapp, and The Clark Sisters Announce The 2024 Reu

May 11, 2024
blog

(CelebrityAccess) — The legendary Kirk Franklin announced he’s teaming up with some of gospel music’s biggest stars for the 2024 leg of The Reunion Tour.

Produced by Live Nation Urban and Undivided Ent, the kicks off on September 6 at Wells Fargo Center in Philadelphia, PA, with 31 additional shows spread across North American before wrapping at the Kia Forum in Los Angeles on November 3rd.

For the tour, Franklin will be joined by co-headliners Yolanda Adams, Fred Hammond, Marvin Sapp, and The Clark Sisters.

Additionally, Kierra Sheard-Kelly will also join the tour as a special guest across all dates.

Tickets will be available starting on May 22 with an artist presale beginning at 10 AM local on May 21st.
