Meaning of Hier encore (Reprise) by Elton John

March 08, 2024
blog

The Elton John song titled 'Hier encore (Reprise)' is a thought-provoking ballad that explores the passage of time, regrets, and the bittersweet nostalgia of looking back on youth. Throughout the song, Elton John and Charles Aznavour reflect on the dreams, hopes, and mistakes of their earlier years. The overall theme of the song revolves around the fleeting nature of time and the significance of the choices we make in life.One standout lyric from the song is: "Hier encore, j'avais vingt ans" (Yesterday, I was twenty years old). This lyric signifies the beginning of the contemplation of the past. It highlights the nostalgia and longing for the energy and innocence of youth. The theme that emerges here is the transformative power of time and the realization that life moves forward faster than we often anticipate.Another significant line from the song is: "J'ai fondé tant d'espoirs, qui se sont envolés" (I have built so many hopes that have flown away). This lyric explores the disappointment that comes with unfulfilled dreams and misplaced expectations. It develops the theme of missed opportunities and reminds us of the importance of seizing the present and not dwelling on past failures.The verse "Ignorant le passé, conjuguant au futur" (Ignoring the past, conjugating the future) brings attention to the dangers of living in the extremes of either past or future, neglecting the present moment. It serves as a reminder to cherish the present and take the time to appreciate the beauty of each passing day. This lyric conveys the theme of gratitude and mindfulness, encouraging us to live in the here and now.A poignant line from the song is: "Mes amours sont mortes, avant que d'exister" (My loves died, before they existed). This expresses the pain of lost relationships and unfulfilled love. It develops the theme of heartbreak and the weight of choices made hastily or without due consideration. It serves as a reminder to cherish and nurture our connections and appreciate the love we have in our lives.Lastly, the song ends with the contemplative question: "À présent mes vingt ans?" (Now, my twenties?). This final lyric emphasizes the fleeting nature of youth and the sentiment of longing for the past. It encapsulates the overall theme of the song, reminding us to embrace the present and appreciate the experiences that shaped who we are today.In conclusion, 'Hier encore (Reprise)' by Elton John and Charles Aznavour transcends time and connects with listeners on an emotional level. The theme of the song, the passage of time, regrets, and the importance of living in the present, is beautifully conveyed through the various thought-provoking lyrics. Each lyric explores different facets of the overarching theme, evoking a sense of reflection and nostalgia. This song encourages us to cherish our past experiences, learn from our mistakes, and appreciate the fleeting moments that make up our lives.
