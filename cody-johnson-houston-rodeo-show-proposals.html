Cody Johnson's Houston Rodeo Concert Had Two Proposals

blog

Cody Johnson fans are sure in love — there were not one, but two proposals (at least!) at his RodeoHouston concert on Friday (March 15).

The first proposal happened during Johnson's meet and greet, before the show even started. A man named Keaton proposed to his girlfriend Savannah right in front of the country star — how could she say no?

She said yes, of course, and the entire room erupted in cheers! The pair have been together since 2013, per the Houston Chronicle.

When Johnson shared a video of the end of the concert — it shows the "On My Way to You" hitmaker and former bull rider riding a horse out of NRG Stadium, where he headlined a 73,000 person show — a fan in the comments section shared a special moment of her own.

"Best show ever! Thank you Jared Alvizo for making my proposal better than I ever imagined," a fan named Amanda writes with video of her engagement. Her husband-to-be, Alvizo, had another concert-goer videotape the proposal from a rows behind them. They were swaying along to Johnson performing "On My Way to You" when Alvizo got on one knee and asked her to be his wife. She too said yes, and fellow concertgoers hugged them and took pictures of the happy couple. Others in the comments section noted that they were nearby and were curious what had happened, since the couple's section was busy with excitement when it went down.

Next up for Taste of Country RISERS star Cody Johnson is headlining tour dates in the United States throughout the spring, with special guests on select dates. He's also hitting the music festival circuit this summer.
