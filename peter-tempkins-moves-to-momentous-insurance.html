Peter Tempkins Moves To Momentous Insurance

July 31, 2024
blog

BRENTWOOD, TN (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — As of January first of this year Partner Peter A. Tempkins’ and his team have parted amicably with DeWitt Stern after 12 years and have set up camp with Momentous Insurance Brokerage. Tempkins’ team consists of Michelle Quintero, Carey Turner, Summer Whitenton, Flor Mejia, Kathleen Miller, Shelly Mutter, and Kate Nolan.

The letterhead may change but their client services and address will remain the same. Moving over to Momentous Insurance Brokerage Inc. includes an average of 20 years of experience insuring music tours, outdoor festivals, live events and professionally managed bands. Momentous is known in both the music and insurance industries as trusted advisors and use this reputation to benefit their clients. They identify potential loss exposures, and help artists develop and implement action plans to reduce those exposures.

Tempkins team at DeWitt insured mostly music tours and festivals. Some of the bigger names are Bonnaroo, AC/DC, The Van Warped Tour, The Jonas Brothers, The White Stripes and John Mayer. All of these tours and more will be following Peter from DeWitt to Momentous Insurance Brokerage Inc.
