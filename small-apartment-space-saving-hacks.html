But let me just put out there that my gripes are actually paired with tips. See, since I’ve been living in perhaps the tiniest apartment in the East Village (with a roommate I might add), I’ve come to master many essential space-saving hacks.

For example, I’ve found a multi-use spice rack (yes, there is such a thing) that takes up absolutely no counter space, a mirror that also functions as a huge unit of a jewelry holder, and a desk that is also a bookshelf and plant holder. All of these things took some time and effort to discover. I have a strict budget—I never spend more than $200 on a single item—and am seriously skeptical about products. After all, I am a shopping editor.
