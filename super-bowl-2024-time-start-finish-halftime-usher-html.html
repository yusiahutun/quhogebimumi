Patrick Mahomes and co are hoping to earn the Kansas City Chiefs their third Super Bowl ring in five years, whilst Mr. Irrelevant Brock Purdy is hoping to cement his name and the San Francisco 49ers in the history books for Super Bowl 58.

The Kansas City Chiefs coached by Andy Reid are hoping to become the first team since 2005 to lift the Lombardi Trophy back to back.

The last team do gain these bragging rights as back to back champions was the New England Patriots led by Tom Brady back in 2005, when they claimed the prestigious title for both Super Bowl 38 and 39.


The game also sees a repeat of Super Bowl 54, where the 49ers fell short to the Chiefs only four years ago.

49ers head coach Kyle Shanahan and his side will be hoping they can take revenge on the Chiefs and claim the Franchise's sixth Super Bowl and their first since 1995.

Here are all the key time stamps you need to know before the game's kick-off on Sunday, February 11.

Patrick Mahomes is hoping to lead the Kansas City Chiefs to their third Super Bowl win in five years 

Brock Purdy is hoping to win his first Super Bowl and earn the 49ers their sixth Super Bowl win

Super Bowl 58 will be held at the Allegiant stadium in Las Vegas on Sunday, February 11

 

What time does the Super Bowl start?

The 2024 Super Bowl will be played on Sunday, February 11 at 6:30pm (EST).

Time:  11:30pm (GMT), 3:30pm local time in Las Vegas

 

 What time is the Super Bowl halftime show? 

 Usher will perform the highly anticipated and watched Super Bowl halftime show.

The 45-year-old is set to take the stage sometime between 8pm and 8:30pm (EST).

Time: Between 1am and 1:30am (GMT), between 5pm and 5:30pm local time.

Usher will perform at this year's Super Bowl halftime show

 

 How long does the Super Bowl last? 

 The Super Bowl usually lasts between 3 to 3 1/2 hours.

 

When will Super Bowl 58 finish?

Although there isn't a set time when the final whistle will blow, we can estimate that Super Bowl 58 will finish in the early hours of Monday, February 8 for UK fans, and around 10pm for those on the east coast of America
