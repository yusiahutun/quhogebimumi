“Hitman” Ricky, 31, was assaulted during a party at the Railway Inn near his house in Hyde last Sunday night.

“There is nobody else to blame apart from himself. He’s got to sort his life out.”

Hatton’s worried family said they would “frogmarch him into rehab”.
