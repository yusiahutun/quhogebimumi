Jason Biggs became famous in 1999 after botching a hookup with Shannon Elizabeth in American Pie.

And now the 36-year-old actor has revealed that his attempt at a threesome with wife Jenny Mollen ended in failure.

Biggs told The View on Thursday that Jenny, 35, had hired a hooker as a birthday present but the situation soon turned comical.

And then just two days later, Biggs went on the show to explain the situation more in depth.

'We are very open people, we are real people,' he confessed. 'This is not a habitual thing on our part. We don’t have a group of prostitutes who come in and out of our house with any regularity.'

But Jenny didn't make it any easier on him, as she found the whole scenario comical: 'My wife found the whole thing to be quite hysterical even while it was happening.'

Although there was one fellow celebrity who was not a fan of the Biggs/Mollen story - Full House alum Candace Cameron.
