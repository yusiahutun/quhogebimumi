Ivanna Anatoliyivna Sakhno (Ukrainian: Іванна Анатоліївна Сахно; born 14 November 1997) is a Ukrainian actress, best known for her roles as Cadet Viktoriya in the science fiction monster film Pacific Rim: Uprising (2018) and the hitwoman, "Nadejda", in the 2018 action comedy film The Spy Who Dumped Me. She had already achieved certain prominence in Ukraine after appearing in 2005 in the first ever Ukrainian-language sitcom, Lesia + Roma, and the 2013 biopic Ivan the Powerful.
