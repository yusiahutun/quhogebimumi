Elle Brooke responds to fighting her sister in 'losers bracket' after first boxing defeat

July 18, 2024
blog

Adult content creator Elle Brooke has distanced herself from a potential face-off against sister Emily.

A potential boxing bout between the pair has been teased after each sibling lost their respective fights in the Kingpyn High Stakes tournament on Saturday. The younger Elle suffered her first career defeat against Jully Oliveira, while older sister Emily lost to Avery '6ar6ie6' Pongracz, both via decision.

Those results meant a losers matchup between the family members was expected to take place in the next phase of the competition. However, it appears that won't be the case.

"I am not fighting my sister Emily Brooke in a losers bracket," tweeted OnlyFans star Elle on Sunday evening. "I plan to take some time away from the gym, focus on creating content and travelling for a while, then come back when there is a fight that makes sense. Plus I need to get back down to my natural weight."

Prior to her defeat against Brazilian Oliveira, the younger member of the Brooke clan was 3-0 in exhibition boxing.

She started her journey almost exactly 12 months ago with a decision win over Andrea-Jane Bunker. Brooke backed that up with a first-round stoppage of Faith Ordway in January 2023 before banking another decision victory over Aleksandra Daniel three months later.

When one Twitter user questioned Brooke's commitment to her contract if she didn't expect to finish competing in the 'losers bracket', she replied that her Kingpyn contract 'expires on August 31'.
