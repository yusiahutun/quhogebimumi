7 Best Moto Boots, Starting at $41

Guysssss. I was just at Coachella and let me tell you, literally one in three people were wearing moto boots. Okay, maybe that’s an inflated statistic (I wasn’t walking around with a clipboard), you could easily spot a few pairs everywhere you looked in the crowd.

I even wore some myself, comfort be damned (when you’re standing for over four hours, any shoe is bound to hurt). In fact, I have tried out a few pairs on this list, so I can lend some expert insight to the best moto boots available, no matter what your budget is. We’re going to mostly stick to pairs that are under $200, but there are plenty of under $100 pairs that are available as well. No matter how much you spend, we’re here to tell you that you don’t need to drop over $2,000 on that pair of Miu Miu boots. Sure they’re cute, but there are so many other moto boots that rival them that are just as cool, and way more affordable.

Here are the best moto boots we’ve found online that you can shop now.

Best Amazon Find: Athlefit Women’s Knee High Riding Boots, $50

  • Aldo Ryderr Boots

    These are the boots I wore all around Coachella. The overloaded hardware gives these boots an extra badass feel, and they were so comfortable. They even withstood the Coachella dust that they were covered in after stomping around for two days, but I ran them under the sink and they were good as new.

  • Madden NYC Women’s Harness Boots

    Madden NYC Women’s Harness Boots

    If you want moto boots that take it down a notch in terms of buckles, these are perfect. These have a foam insole for added comfort and longer wear, plus the chunky sole is perfect for stomping through the city streets.

    If you join Walmart Plus, you can score free shipping!

  • Wild Fable Rebel Tall Moto Boots with Memory Foam Insole 

    Rebel Tall Moto Boots with Memory Foam Insole 

    Did you know that most shoes from Target feature a memory foam insole? That’s reason enough to buy although the affordable prices don’t hurt either. These are just $50 and come in half sizes.

  • Athlefit Women's Knee High Riding Boots

    Add leg warmers or knee-high socks for an even cooler look, plus, they’ll probably be more comfortable if you plan on wearing these boots for a longer amount of time. These come in black and brown and run in half sizes!

  • Dream Pairs Square Toe Motorcycle Boots

    Dream Pairs Square Toe Motorcycle Boots

    Just because we’re talking about moto boots doesn’t mean they have to be black. I love this silver pair from Dream Pairs that looks pretty much exactly like the Miu Miu pair in terms of buckles. I’ve been wearing this pair and they add instant edge to any ‘fit. I love wearing these with dresses to make sure the look still looks a bit grunge.

  • Dolce Vita Evi Boots

    Evi Boots

    On Sale 39% off

    This is another pair I can attest for personally. I just wore these out on Friday and got so many compliments in the line for the bar bathroom (you know, where fashion really counts). My favorite thing about these boots is how luxe and high quality they look and feel, while somehow being on sale for under $200. These are made of leather, so if you want to invest in a pair that’ll last, I’d go with these. The silver detail on the toe is the perfect touch.

  • Azalea Wang Buckled Up Moto Boot

    Azalea Wang Buckled Up Moto Boot

    Want to stand out from the moto boot crowd? Grab these silver stunners at Urban Outfitters. They are perfect for telling the aliens you’re on their side, or you know, just for elevating a cute outfit.
