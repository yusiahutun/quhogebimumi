Celebrity birthdays 14 May: Cate Blanchett, Olly Murs and Martin Garrix

June 17, 2024
blog

We hope they all have a special day!

    We are wishing a very happy birthday to everyone who is celebrating their day of birth today – 14 May. At HELLO!, we are taking a look at some of the famous faces who will be enjoying their birthdays with their loved ones - and plenty of cake and champagne hopefully! From a Hollywood actress to a very popular presenter in the UK, take a look to see who's celebrating today…

    Cate Blanchett turns 49

    Happy Birthday to award-winning actress Cate Blanchett! The mother-of-four has been busy in the lead-up to her birthday, attending the star-studded Cannes Film Festival in her role as Jury President. On Saturday, the Australian actress led the Cannes Women's March, linking arms with 82 other female stars to highlight the #MeToo movement. "Women are not a minority in the world, yet the current state of the industry says otherwise," Cate said of the issue, adding, "We stand together on these steps today as a symbol of our determination to change and progress." The star will continue to champion the empowerment of women by starring in the upcoming all-female movie alongside other Hollywood leading ladies including Sandra Bullock, Rihanna, Anne Hathaway and Helena Bonham Carter.

    Olly Murs turns 34

    Happy Birthday to singer Olly Murs! It has been a successful year for the former star, who co-owns and plays for non-league football team Coggeshall Town FC. "I love being a part of this. Aside from my music, when I am here with the boys I am just one of the lads," says Olly of the venture. "These are moments I will cherish for the rest of my life." Earlier this month, Olly kicked off his birthday celebrations by enjoying a night out with pals including Mark Wright at The Highlight Room in L.A.

    Martin Garrix turns 22

    Happy Birthday to superstar DJ Martin Garrix! The hitmaker is only turning 22 years old, but has already built a successful music career! After learning to play the guitar at the age of just eight, Martin has gone on to found his own record label STMPD RCRDS, was named the world's top DJ in DJ Mag's annual poll, and is about to embark on his very own world tour. We can't wait to see what else is in store for Martin
