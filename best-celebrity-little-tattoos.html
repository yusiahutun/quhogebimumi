The best celebrity little tattoos to inspire your next ink - see photos

April 09, 2024
blog

It goes without saying that tattoos are a huge commitment. A moment of ink should really require a lifetime of thought, right? No pressure or anything, but it's going to be on your body forever.

Luckily for those of us who are slightly more hesitant when it comes to getting a tattoo, there's really no need to jump in at the deep end and cover yourself from head to toe. There are plenty of really stylish dainty designs that can have a beautiful but subtle impact, and we're looking to the likes of Kaia Gerber, Kendall Jenner and Hailey Bieber for inspiration.

MORE: 9 royals who have surprised us with their tattoos and body art: From Princess Eugenie to Kate Middleton

Hello! Fashion has picked out the best celebrity little tattoos to inspire your next ink:

Bella Hadid

 

 

@jonboytattoo

Bella Hadid has a delicate rosebud just above her left elbow.

Julia Fox 

Julia Fox has two f-holes, as typically seen on stringed instruments, tattoed on her back.

Kaia Gerber

Kaia Gerber has an intricate anatomical heart tattooed in red ink on her right forearm.

Hailey Bieber

 

 

@mr.k_tattoo

Hailey Bieber has a cursive letter "J" on her ring finger referring to the first name of her husband, Justin Bieber, and a small celestial motif.

Selena Gomez

 

 

@bangbangnyc

Selena Gomez has the name of one of her albums, "Rare" tattooed just below her right earlobe. The ink also nods to the name of her makeup line, Rare Beauty.

Sienna Miller 

Sienna Miller has a cluster of three five-point stars on her right shoulder.

Vanessa Hudgens

Vanessa Hudgens has a coloured sunflower tattoo located on her ribcage.

Kendall Jenner

Kendall Jenner has the word "Meow" tattooed on the inside of her bottom lip. The model got the tattoo after a night of drinking with her friends. Purr-fection. 

Rihanna 

 

 

Rihanna has the words "Never a failure, always a lesson" tattooed backwards on her collarbone so that she can read the mantra as she looks in the mirror.

Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

Rosie Huntington-Whiteley has a simple five-point star tattooed on the outside of her right ankle.

Zoë Kravitz

 

 

Actress Zoë Kravitz has a flock of flying birds tattooed on her right shoulder.

Ariana Grande 

Global singing sensation Ariana Grande has heaps of tiny tats that are well worth a mention. On her right hand alone she has the name of her dog "Toulouse", the sun, moon, stars, Saturn and various botanical designs to name a few.

Miley Cyrus 

Miley Cyrus is another star who is constantly adding to her ink collection. On her left arm she has the letters "WA" tattooed inside a heart and well as the word "Moo" on her forearm and on her right fingers she has an eye, a watermelon slice, a heart, an alien and the word "BAD". 

Lady Amelia Windsor 

Stylish royal Lady Amelia Windsor has a small number of tattoos, including one on her ribcage, a tiger on her shoulder blade and several running across the insides of her arms.
