Juno Temple was born to be a star.

With a producer mother and film director father, it comes as no surprise that the actress is a starlet whose fame is firmly in ascendance.

Her career started off with a bang with her first acting role being alongside Cate Blanchett and Dame Judi Dench in the Oscar-nominated Notes on a Scandal in 2006 and since then there has been no stopping her.

Juno Temple broke into the film world at such a young age, but judging by her success so far, there is plenty more to come

From her most recent role in The Dark Knight to appearances in Atonement and chick-flick St. Trinian's, the 23-year-old British star already has a sparkling CV to rival actors twice her age.

Speaking in an exclusive interview with Asos magazine, Juno said: 'I've had extraordinary experiences. I don't want to stop, ever.

'I'm going to fight for it as long as possible and whether that leads to big things or little things, it's all big to me, because I'm working.'

The natural and chic blonde actress grew up in England, flitting between Somerset and west London but has called Los Angeles home for the last four years.

Known for her quirky and edgy style, Juno loves thrift stores but she isn't impartial to an occasional Miu Miu or Alexander McQueen dress

Not only has living in tinsel town boosted her career, it has also cast a magical spell over her love life and she has fallen hard for fellow actor Michael Angarano.

'Since moving to L.A. I've fallen in love with someone really magical.

Juno is this month's Asos magazine cover star and she revealed all about her love of fashion and a certain L.A. actor

'And I never thought I'd say this, because I love rain, but actually, waking up to sunshine does wonders for your brain,' she said.

Although she is making great headway taking the acting world by storm, she would love to dabble in the world of fashion and designing.

Known for her quirky and thrifty style (she wore a $17 dress to the Oscars), Juno is feminine beneath it all, owning a staggering 80 sets of Agent Provocateur underwear.

'It makes me feel like a woman and I love being a woman, I think you should secretly indulge in that every day,' she told the magazine.

She also counts Miu Miu, Vivienne Westwood and Alexander McQueen as some of her favourite designers and was honoured to be one of four actresses to be invited to the Miu Miu show for Paris Fashion Week.

Behind the grungy look likes a true girlie girl who spends forty five minutes prepping her face and body before bed and feels like a 'princess' when she steps out for a red carpet event.

And with an array of films already under her belt, there is undoubtedly plenty more to come from the budding star.
