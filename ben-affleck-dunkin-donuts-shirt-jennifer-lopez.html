Ben Affleck Wears Dunkin' Donuts Shirt While Out With Jennifer Lopez

March 11, 2024
blog

Find someone who loves you as much as Ben Affleck loves Dunkin’ Donuts.

The Air actor and director was spotted wearing his favorite Dunkin’ T-shirt while out this week in the Hamptons with stylish wife Jennifer Lopez. The tee, from the Dunkin’ collaboration with Boston sports fan brand Sully’s, is black with the words Believe in Boston in the doughnut chain’s famous orange and pink bubble letters. Affleck wore it with beige slacks, tan-and-white Air Jordans, and black-tinted aviator sunglasses.

Lopez also kept it casual for the day, in an airy white V-neck tee, loose ripped jeans, white sneakers, a raffia and brown leather handbag, and rose-tinted aviator sunglasses. She had her hair up in a messy bun and was seen gazing lovingly at her husband.

Splash News

This is far from the first time Affleck has shown his inexplicable adoration for and commitment to Dunkin’. In February, he was seen working the window at a Dunkin’ Donuts drive-thru in Massachusetts for a commercial for the chain. A handful of lucky customers got their orders taken by the Deep Water star, and at one point, J.Lo even showed up to support him.

The commercial was released in April, and the wait was truly worth it. In it, Affleck is seen arriving at Dunkin’ for his daily iced coffee and doughnut and is hilariously mistaken for longtime friend Matt Damon by the staff. (Watch it here.)

Affleck’s love of the chain has even turned glamorous bling-cup queen J.Lo into a Dunkin’ girl. Some weeks after their wedding, the Afflecks were seen stopping by a Dunkin’ in Los Angeles’s Brentwood neighborhood, where they bought coffee drinks and snacks for themselves and their kids.

“I love that he’s introducing her to his culture,” one Twitter user joked upon seeing photos of the couple at the doughnut-and-coffee shop on another occasion.

“JLo taken to Dunkin. Rihanna taken to Barcade. The baddest bitch will really do anything for love,” another wrote.
