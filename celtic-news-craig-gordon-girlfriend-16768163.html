Craig Gordons girlfriend in Instagram celebration as Celtic win eighth straight league

October 03, 2024
blog

The Hoops had an impressive 3-0 victory over Aberdeen to win the title at Pittodrie this afternoon.

With the Scottish League Cup already in the bag, Celtic – still unbeaten under interim boss Neil Lennon – will now have their sights set on an unprecedented domestic triple treble.

Among those celebrating was goalkeeper Craig Gordon, 36, and his girlfriend Summer Harl.

Gordon wasn’t in the squad for the game but he took to Instagram to share some celebration pics from the dressing room and the field.

He said: “Champions again. Celtic – delighted for the team and all involved.”

The post was liked by his girlfriend Summer Harl, a former Celtic television host, who shared her own celebration on Instagram.

She shared a GIF which read: “Congratulations Celtic, SPL eight in a row champions.”

The loved-up duo are keen sightseers, after sharing their adventure in Dublin earlier this year.

They crawled the Irish capital’s bars and shared snaps of themselves hand-in-hand.

Summer is a blogger based in Edinburgh.

Together, they regularly share smitten snaps on their Instagram accounts to the joy of followers.

On the pitch, Gordon has found himself out of favour at Celtic Park, with Scott Bain preferred between the sticks.

Celtic are now champions after their win against Aberdeen put them 12 points ahead of Rangers who have three games left to Celtic’s two.

James Wilson twice struck the woodwork for the Dons before Mikael Lustig dived to head home Callum McGregor's angled cross five minutes before the break to put the Bhoys in front.

Jozo Simunovic, the match-winner against Kilmarnock last week, doubled Celtic's lead in the 53rd minute with an excellent header from McGregor's corner.

Odsonne Edoaurd put the icing on the cake two minutes from time after being teed up by Tom Rogic to claim his 21st goal of the season.
