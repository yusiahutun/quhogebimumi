Does a spine tattoo hurt?

Yes, a spine tattoo is considered among the most painful tattoos. It hurts because the bones are just under the skin, and the vibrations from the tattoo machine will radiate through the skeleton.

Is it safe to get a tattoo on your spine?

Yes, however, those with back or spine problems that potentially require surgery should refrain from getting a tattoo on their back, especially their lower back, as this could further damage the spine.
