Mom's sons take on decorating their Christmas tree themselves after their dad's cancer diagnosis

August 15, 2024
blog

A mother who put off decorating for the holidays after her husband was diagnosed with cancer was moved to tears by the Christmas tree her young sons decorated themselves. 

Sheena Schuber, 38, from Tinley Park, Illinois, opened up the heartwarming moment in an interview with Today, saying her husband Nick Schuber, 35, was diagnosed with stage 4 cancer on November 5. 

The mom explained that her family 'usually goes all out for Christmas,' and Nick even dresses up as Santa Claus, but she 'didn't have the drive' this holiday season.  

That's when her sons A.J., seven, and Michael, three, decided to take over.
