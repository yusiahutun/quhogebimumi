Gene Chizik reveals why he went on SPRING BREAK with his daughters

April 05, 2024
blog

As a self-confessed 'helicopter dad', Gene Chizik is well known for taking a somewhat over-protective attitude towards his twin daughters. 

But the former Auburn football coach, 56, has taken things to a new level by accompanying the 20-year-olds on their Spring Break holiday.

Chizik and his wife Jonna flew to Mexico with Landry and Kennedy before renting a hotel a mile away from where they were staying with their classmates.

Chizik and his wife Jonna flew to Mexico with Landry and Kennedy before renting a hotel a mile away from where they were staying with their classmates. The former coach posted this holiday snap of him with his two daughters on March 10

Chizik posted this tweet next to the image above. He confesses to being a 'helicopter dad' who wants to check his daughters are ok all the time

The short distance did not stop Chizik going along to a foam party and posting a video of the raucous occasion on Twitter - all to educate parents back home about what their children were up to, he said. 

The father-of-three explained his reasoning in an amusing interview with AL.com's Lauren Sisler. 

'I am a helicopter dad, hovering over them all the time making sure that everything's good,' he said.
