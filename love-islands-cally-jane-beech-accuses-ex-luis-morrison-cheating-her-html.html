After sparking a romance on Love Island back in 2015, the couple had a turbulent on-off relationship before finally calling it quits in September.

While Luis was one of the original Islanders on the series, Cally, a then-23-year-old dental nurse, entered later on.

In the villa, they rekindled their flame and eventually finished in fourth place on the ITV2 show.

After a party-laden start to their romance, things soon changed in the Beech-Morrisson camp when they welcomed a daughter, Vienna, in May last year.

Just ten weeks after Vienna's birth, the couple split after Cally hinted in a public statement that Luis had been unfaithful.

In December 2017, they confirmed they were back together, explaining: 'For the record yes we are together. Had a really tough year, but couldn't be more ready for 2018. Onwards and upwards.'

However the pair were unable to sort out their differences and decided to call time on their romance with Cally returning to her home in Hull with Vienna, while Luis stayed in London.
