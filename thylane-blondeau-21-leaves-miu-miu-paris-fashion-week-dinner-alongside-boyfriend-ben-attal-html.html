Thylane Blondeau, 21, leaves Miu Miu Paris Fashion Week dinner alongside boyfriend Ben Attal

May 30, 2024
blog

Thylane Blondeau looked radiant as she left the Miu Miu Paris Fashion Week dinner alongside her boyfriend Ben Attal on Tuesday night. 

The French model, 21, wore an all-black ensemble, including a ribbed black top and floor-length coat to keep out the chill after the dinner and after-show party at Gigi Parisas on the final day of Paris Fashion Week.

Thylane, who was branded the 'most beautiful girl in the world' when she was six, was accompanied by her beau - French actor Ben Attal, 25. 

She kept her make-up natural to accentuate her stunning features, while the top section of her hair was neatly plaited, as Ben also opted for a one-shade colour palette. 

She wore no jewellery for her stripped-back look as the gorgeous couple headed back from their evening out.  

Natural beauty: Thylane Blondeau, 21, looked radiant as she left the Miu Miu dinner at Paris Fashion Week on Tuesday night

Happy couple: Also in attendance was her beau Ben Attal, 25, who she has been dating since 2020

Earlier in the day, Thylane gave a smouldering look as she dressed for the Miu Miu's womenswear fall/winter 2023/24 show. 

An after-party to celebrate the show saw the likes of Tina Kunakey, Kylie Minogue and Amelia Gray Hamlin. 

A star-studded line-up also attended to watch the presentation, including Alexa Chung, Jessica Alba and married couple Nicola Peltz and Brooklyn Beckham. 

Miu Miu described its latest collection as 'an antidote to meaningless novelty' as they closed Paris Fashion Week on Tuesday afternoon.

The presentation gave followers of the popular brand an opportunity to preview its forthcoming silhouettes ahead of the new season.

Thylane is the daughter of football player Patrick Blondeau, and rocketed to fame in 2007 at the age of six after being named the 'most beautiful girl in the world'.

It's an accolade that she's achieved twice, as 11 years later Thylane once again cinched the coveted title, coming in top of TC Candler's 2018 annual awards list.

The awards, which began in 1990, pride themselves on being among the most 'diverse' beauty awards.

Keeping it simple: Thylane opted for an all-black ensemble, natural make-up palette and no jewellery

The most beautiful girl in the world: Thylane gained the title when she was a child and is now a model

High fashion: The designer gave a smouldering look as she attended the Miu Miu womenswear fall winter collection on Tuesday

Taking to Instagram at the time, a delighted Thylane thanked the competition for the award, admitting that she 'couldn't believe it'.

She wrote: 'I just can’t believe it myself, thanks so much @tccandler and everyone who voted.

'I never thought I was once number one! I’m thankful to all of you I really hope you're having great holidays and spend nice Christmas with your family... all the best to all of you guys lots of love (sic)'.

Thylane walked for Jean Paul Gaultier at the age of four and then later, aged 10, became the youngest ever model to pose for Vogue Paris.

The designer has modelled for designers including Dolce and Gabbana, L'Oreal and Versace. In 2018 she founded her own clothing brand, Heaven May. 

She has been dating Ben, son of Yvan Atall and Charlotte Gainsbourg, since 2020 and was previously in a relationship with French DJ Milane Meritte.  

In October 2021 she revealed she had emergency surgery after an ovarian cyst 'exploded', sharing photos from her hospital bed 

Sharing photos of herself in her hospital gown and an image of her scan, Thylane went on to explain that she began experiencing pain again three months after her first surgery.

She said she saw three different gynecologists and went to more than four radiology centers - and each time was told nothing was wrong. 

But after the pain became too much to bear, she had an emergency operation to remove a cyst.
