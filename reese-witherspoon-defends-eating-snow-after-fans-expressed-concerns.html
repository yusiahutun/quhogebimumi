Reese Witherspoon 'defends' eating snow after fans expressed concerns

August 23, 2024
blog

Reese Witherspoon was like an excited kid at the sight of snow, so much so, she decided to taste some by making a Chococinno. However, unbeknownst to her, her comments were filled with fans telling her it wasn’t “safe to eat,” which she has now responded to.

Instead of just going out to make a snowman, the Hollywood star opted for something more inventive, as she made a tasty treat from the fresh snow outside. Although, we don’t think any fans will be following the recipe anytime soon…

Reese Witherspoon ‘defends’ eating snow

The 47-year-old wasn’t unaware of her TikTok comments, which were filled with concerned fans as she scooped heaps of snow into a coffee mug, and ate it. “No no no.. snow is not made to eat.. u can get seriously sick,” wrote one.

Another penned: “Am I the only one concerned that she scooped it off of her car.” However, she’s returned to the app, and lived to tell the tale.

The actress seemed completely unaware of concerns, and even melted the snow, upon some fan requests, which turned into clear water.

In other, she stated: “OK, so we’re kind of in a category of ‘You only live once,'” before revealing the treat was “delicious”.

The Water for Elephants then went on to say she didn’t “grow up drinking filtered water,” before joking, “Maybe that’s why I’m like this.”

The star reunites with ‘sister’ Jennifer Aniston

As well as her endless iconic roles in movies, Reese is well-known for playing Rachel’s younger sister Jill in the hit TV show, Friends.

Just this week, the duo sent Instagram wild as they reunited on the Critics Choice Awards Red Carpet.

The two both stunned in long black gowns, as Reese wrote in the caption: “Always the best time with my forever sister, partner, and friend @jenniferaniston.”

Fans rushed to the comments, as one wrote: “You both look fabulous!”

“The green sisters forever and ever,” penned one Friends fan.
