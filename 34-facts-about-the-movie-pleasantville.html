FAQs

Q: What is the plot of Pleasantville?

A: Pleasantville tells the story of two teenagers, David and Jennifer, who are transported into a black-and-white 1950s sitcom world called Pleasantville. As they navigate this idyllic town, they begin to disrupt its perfect existence by introducing change, challenging the residents’ conformity, and bringing color to the monochrome society.

Q: Who are the main characters in Pleasantville?

A: The main characters in Pleasantville are David and Jennifer, played by Tobey Maguire and Reese Witherspoon, who are siblings transported into the world of Pleasantville. Other notable characters include George Parker (played by William H. Macy), Bud Parker (played by Jeff Daniels), and Betty Parker (played by Joan Allen).

A: Pleasantville explores themes such as conformity versus individuality, the power of change and personal growth, the role of art in society, and the dangers of nostalgia. The film also touches upon issues of racism, sexism, and social progress.
