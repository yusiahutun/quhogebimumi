Brie Larson Broke Into Tears Meeting Jennifer Lopez on the Golden Globes Red Carpet

April 02, 2024
blog

The Golden Globes are always a big night for Hollywood stars, but last night’s 81st show was especially significant for Brie Larson, for a sentimental reason.

While speaking to Entertainment Tonight on the red carpet ahead of the ceremony at the Beverly Hilton in L.A., the Marvels star had a flustered reaction to seeing and meeting Jennifer Lopez.

“Oh, my god. Oh, my. I’m gonna cry,” Larson said, emotion audible in her voice and appearing to hold back tears. “I actually can’t. She’s the reason I wanted to be an actor. I can’t. No, I’m gonna cry!”

As Lopez ascended the steps behind her, Larson looked on in awe. “You mean so much to me,” Larson told J.Lo as the stars made eye contact.

“Hi, how are you?” Lopez asked, greeting Larson with a hug.

“I’m such a huge fan,” Larson told her. “I saw Selena and it made me want to be an actor, and you’ve always meant so much to me.”

“Oh my god, don’t make me cry,” replied Lopez, who made history starring in the 1997 biopic about the Tejano music sensation—her first leading role—becoming the first Latina actor to earn over $1 million for a film.

Getty Images

“This has been a dream of mine, so thank you so much,” Larson told the actor, singer, and dancer. “Your work ethic is so important, thank you. … I’ve been wanting to say that to you for a long time.”

“Thank you. Aw, you’re sweet,” Lopez responded, holding Larson’s hands close to herself, before appearing to wipe away one of her own tears.

As Larson calmed herself down and prepared to leave the interview, she added, “Whew, okay. I’m gonna have to go do a shot of tequila or something. … Thank you for that. I don’t know if our interview is done, but it has to be.”
