Blake Lively Can/t Stop Wearing This Rare Blue Denim Chanel Bag

June 11, 2024
blog

Blake Lively loves a high-low moment.

When she’s not on the red carpet or dining with her famous friends, the Gossip Girl star keeps it low-key with her style—but she always finds a way to add a pop of luxury into even the most laid-back look. Case in point, the actor has been wearing and re-wearing the same rare vintage Chanel bag with her latest off-duty fall fits.

The piece is a boxy blue denim crossbody bag with a quilted top, a golden CC buckle, and the house’s iconic golden chain strap. (Similar styles are available at Sotheby’s.)

Lively wore the bag in late October, on a pre-Halloween stroll in New York City with husband Ryan Reynolds. Then, her outfit consisted of a grayish-blue hoodie, classic blue jeans, a cozy navy blue coat, and chunky brown shoes. She accessorized with brown tortoiseshell glasses and seemed to wear no makeup.

Reynolds contrasted his wife’s all-blue outfit by choosing an all-gray look. It was composed of a gray collared jacket layered over a simple white tee, sleek gray trousers, white sneakers with gray socks, and a gray beanie. He, too, accessorized with brown tortoiseshell shades.

Ignat/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

The mom of four again wore the bag yesterday, on a solo walk in the city. Her fall ensemble was composed of a dark blue turtleneck sweater, a brown plaid Madewell blazer that she cinched at the waist, straight-leg blue jeans, and brown lace-up boots. Lively was photographed with her AirPods in, and seemed to wear no additional accessories or makeup.
