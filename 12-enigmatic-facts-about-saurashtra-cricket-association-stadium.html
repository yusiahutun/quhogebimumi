The Largest Stadium in Saurashtra

Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium holds the distinction of being the largest stadium in the Saurashtra region of Gujarat. With a seating capacity of over 28,000 spectators, it provides an electrifying atmosphere for cricket matches.

A Venue for International Cricket

This stadium has hosted various international cricket matches, including Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 Internationals (T20Is). It has witnessed thrilling encounters between top cricketing nations, providing unforgettable moments for fans.

A Memorable Debut Match

The stadium made its Test debut in 2016 when India played against England. This historic match marked a significant milestone in the stadium’s journey and showcased the world-class facilities it offers.

The Home of the Gujarat Lions

Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium served as the home ground for the Gujarat Lions, one of the teams in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The roaring crowd and the vibrant ambiance added to the excitement of IPL matches held at this iconic venue.

A Batting Paradise

The pitch at Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium is known for being batsman-friendly, offering a true bounce and excellent pace. This makes it an ideal ground for high-scoring matches and exhilarating batting performances.

An Architectural Marvel

The stadium’s architectural design showcases modern aesthetics, combining functionality with elegance. Its eye-catching facade and state-of-the-art facilities make it a visually stunning landmark in Rajkot.

A Hub for Cricketing Talent

Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium has played a crucial role in nurturing local cricketing talent. It has been a breeding ground for many talented players who have gone on to represent both the state of Gujarat and the Indian national cricket team.

A Soaring Pavilion Stand

The pavilion stand at this stadium is a sight to behold. With its towering height and mesmerizing view, it offers an exceptional vantage point for spectators to soak in the cricketing action while enjoying the breathtaking panoramic views.

World-Class Facilities

Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium is the home ground for the Gujarat cricket team, which represents the state in domestic cricket tournaments. The team has enjoyed tremendous success on this hallowed ground, achieving both individual and collective milestones.
