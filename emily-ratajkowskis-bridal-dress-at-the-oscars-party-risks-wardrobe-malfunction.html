Emily Ratajkowski's Bridal dress at the Oscars party risks wardrobe malfunction

August 16, 2024
blog

Emily Ratajkowski stunned as always in her risky dress at the famous Vanity Fair Oscars party and held her nerve as a slip-up could have been imminent at any time. However, she survived (publicly) but still left little to the imagination with her brave fashion choices.

The Oscars are the most important night in Hollywood, and the Vanity Fair after-party is where we see some of the best fashion looks of the year. The A-Listers come out in force hoping to make an impression, and that’s exactly what Em Rata did with her daring dress.

Emily Ratajkowski’s daring dress at the Oscars after party

The model wore a long white dress, but the details were anything but pure. The bridal-style gown, from French brand Jacquemus, known for their tiny bags, had enough room for some skin to be on show.

Em decided to ditch the bra, showing as much skin as she could while posing sideways for the pap cameras. She had nothing to hide but everything to show!

The backless gown floated across her body, showcasing the mom’s incredible body.

The 32-year-old was ‘matching’ with reality TV queen, Kim Kardashian who also wore a long white gown. However, the SKIMs owner avoided a potential wardrobe malfunction as the top of her dress appeared higher up.

Although we think she slayed, many users on X, formerly known as Twitter, weren’t too keen on the look. “Did Kanye design this dress? It’s horrible….,” wrote one.

A second claimed: “She looks so uncomfortable.”

The model put on an even more daring display at Beyoncé and Jay-Z’s after-party

The party, or the ‘revelations’ didn’t stop there for the model, as she headed off to Beyoncé and Jay-Z’s after-party afterward, leaving even less to the imagination.

In a complete switch-up from her first outfit, Em opted for a black gown, which this time, was completely see-through.

Again, she opted for no bra, meaning on her top half, everything was on show. However, she didn’t go fully commando and opted for the safe bet of black underwear.

If you’ve got it, flaunt it!
