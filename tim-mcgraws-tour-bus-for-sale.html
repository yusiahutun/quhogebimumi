Tim McGraw's Old Tour Bus Is for Sale

June 15, 2024
blog

The 1995 Eagle 45′ tour bus is basically a mini house, featuring 11 bunks, a full-size fridge and kitchen with several cabinets and suitable counter space, along with a marble-wall bathroom and TV area in the back of the vehicle. The retro-style bus also includes leather seating/couches and dark lighting that give it a vintage feel.

In terms of mechanics, the 23-year-old bus has a Detroit 60 series motor, five-speed engine retarder and a new generator; and it provides ample heating and air conditioning. The country star sold it to Vinnie Paul of heavy metal bands Hellyeah and Pantera. The bus costs $45,000 and is available on eBay.

McGraw and his fellow superstar wife Faith Hill released their first-ever duets album in 2017, The Rest of Our Life, which had a winning debut at No. 1 on Billboard's Top Country Albums chart and hit No. 2 on the Billboard 200. The beloved couple has extended their successful Soul2Soul Tour into 2018, adding 25 more dates to the trek. The second leg kicks off May 31 in Richmond, Va. and continues through the summer before wrapping up July 22 in Sacramento, Calif.

Their multi-date 2017 tour landed them at No. 1 on a variety of Pollstar's rankings including Average Grossing Country Tour (North America) and Average Grossing Co-Headline Tour (U.S.). They're also set to co-headline London's O2 Arena during C2C: Country to Country Festival on March 9 as part of the Soul2Soul Tour.
