'1923' Fans Call Julia Schlaepfer &quot;Breathtaking&quot; in Sultry Gown

July 04, 2024
blog

On 1923, Julia Schlaepfer stars free-spirited Alex, a young woman who gets swept up in a passionate love affair with Spencer Dutton (Brandon Sklenar) that spans from Africa to Europe. The character steals the show with her incredible period clothing, from slinky slips to structured menswear to intricate flapper gowns. Julia seems to share the same fun-loving approach to fashion, and fans can't get enough of her ever-changing looks.

This time, the 28-year-old actress stepped out for the Paramount upfronts. According to AdWeek, the streaming network skipped the typical upfront format for more "intimate gatherings" for clients and partners.

Julia shared photos of her look with her fans on Instagram. She sits on a park bench wearing a gorgeous cream dress with a flair skirt and leather bra top by Marina Moscone paired with a clutch by Serpui.

Fans showed their appreciation for her look with comments like "absolutely breathtaking," "gorgeous," "OMG goddess," and "stunningly beautiful." Julia's costars chimed in with their comments. Michelle Randolph (Elizabeth) posted drooling face emojis, and Aminah Nieves (Teonna Rainwater) called Julia an "ANGEL."

One fan called Julia a "chameleon," with different fashion looks at every appearance. She proved that point with a recent appearance on the MSNBC show Morning Joe, where she wore a black-and-gray plaid dress with a white collar and an oversize black bow in her ponytail.

We'll be looking for more fashion moments as we wait for news about 1923's second season.
