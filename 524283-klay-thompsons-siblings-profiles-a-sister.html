Who is Klay Thompson?

Klay Alexander Thompson is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA). At the time of writing, the athlete is a four-time NBA champion with the Warriors (2015, 2017, 2018, 2022). He has also won gold twice with the United States national team at the 2014 World Cup and the 2016 Olympics.

Klay was born to Julie and Mychal Thompson on 8 February 1990 in Los Angeles, California, USA. He is 33 years old (as of 2023), and his zodiac sign is Aquarius.

Klay's father, originally from the Bahamas, is a former NBA player who played for the Portland Trailblazers, the San Antonio Spurs and the Los Angeles Lakers. Klay's mother was a collegiate volleyball player and track and field athlete.

Does Klay Thompson have a sister? No, he doesn't. The NBA star comes from a family of boys. In addition, just like their parents, Klay's siblings are professional athletes.

Klay Thompson's siblings

How many brothers does Klay Thompson have? The NBA player has two brothers, Mychel and Trayce. Here is a detailed breakdown of their biographies.

Mychel Thompson

Mychel is a former basketball player and the oldest brother of Klay Thompson. He was born 1 June 1988 in Los Angeles, California, USA. Mychel is 35 years old (as of 2023), and his zodiac sign is Gemini.

Mychel attended high school at Santa Margarita Catholic, where, after completion, he joined and played for Stoneridge Prep for a year. He then enrolled at Pepperdine College from 2007 to 2011.

Mychel's basketball career began after he went undrafted in the 2011 NBA draft. He eventually joined the Erie BayHawks in the NBA Development League. Mychel briefly signed with the Cleveland Cavaliers in 2011 but was waived after just five games.

Mychel also had stints with the New York Knicks and Sioux Falls Skyforce in 2012 and 2013, respectively. Notably, he played a significant part in his career with the Santa Cruz Warriors, winning the D-League championship in 2015. Mychel retired from professional basketball in 2018.

Trayce Thompson

Trayce Nikolas Thompson is an American professional baseball outfielder for the Chicago White Sox of Major League Baseball (MLB). Are Klay and Trayce Thompson brothers? Yes, they are. Trayce is the younger brother of NBA star Klay Thompson.

Trayce was born on 15 March 1991 in Los Angeles, California, USA. He is 32 years old (as of 2023), and his zodiac sign is Pisces.

Trayce attended Santa Margarita Catholic High School in Rancho Santa Margarita, California. He had committed to join UCLA to play college baseball. However, Trayce changed his mind after the Chicago White Sox selected him in the second round of the 2009 MLB Draft.

Trayce began his MLB career with the Chicago White Sox in 2009. He had successful stints with the Los Angeles Dodgers, Oakland Athletics, Chicago Cubs, San Diego Padres, and Detroit Tigers. In 2023, Trayce returned to the Chicago White Sox for his third stint.
