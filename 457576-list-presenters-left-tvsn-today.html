Justine was one of the original TVSN presenters. In May 2019, fans of the network awoke to the sad news that she was leaving the station. Justine had worked for TVSN for more than ten years, and she was a fan favourite.

She left because she wanted to spend more time with her family and explore more opportunities closer to home. So what is Justine Diana doing? Since she left TVSN, she is busy selling items such as jewellery online. She is the owner of the Justine Diana Shop.

2. Carla King-Turner

Carla is one of the TVSN presenters who have left. She is a TV presenter, content creator, and brand affiliate. Carla has a wealth of knowledge in presenting live TV, commercials, web clips, lifestyle shows, and branding videos.

Carla had been part of TVSN for more than 15 years, and she contributed to the success of the company both on and off-air. 3. Nikki Vincent

Nikki was a fan favourite on TVSN, and she had been part of the TVSN family for many years. However, in May 2019, the company announced on Facebook and Instagram that she had resigned from her position.

The company did not disclose the reason why she was leaving, but it seems that she left to focus on her personal interests. So, what is Nikki Vincent doing? She is currently serving as the CEO of Beyounique & Beyoutiful.

4. Mark Pickett

Mark was also one of the original TVSN presenters, and he had been part of the TVSN family from the very start. He is a sales and marketing expert with extensive experience across multiple channels. Mark left the station in December 2019 after serving in many varied roles.

When he joined the station, he was charged with the responsibility of hiring and training studio staff. Later on, he was assigned to work as a presenter. At the time of leaving, he was one of the most loved presenters.

He is fondly remembered for his extensive knowledge of jewellery and his enthusiasm both on and off-air. At the moment, he is occupied with his role as the founder and CEO of G Marketing International, which is a global marketing business.

5. Emma Grant Williams

Emma is an actor, presenter, and voice actor based in Sydney, Australia. Her work encompasses film, theatre, television, and radio. Emma holds a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Psychology/Drama from Monash University. Her presenting experience spans international broadcasts, lifestyle editorials, vox pops, interviews, roadshows, and global media corporations.
