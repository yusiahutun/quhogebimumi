Jos Canseco's daughter Josie poses for Playboy shoot and is named Miss June 2016

February 13, 2024

In the grand tradition of celebrity kids trying to follow in their parents' famous footsteps, Josie Canseco is attempting to live up to her MLB star dad's name by forging a modeling career.

But with shoots for Teen Vogue and Town & Country already filling up her portfolio, the 19-year-old deciding to go a bit more risqué with her latest gig: stripping down for Playboy.

The daughter of retired outfielder José Canseco has been named Miss June 2016, and took a series of seriously saucy shots to live up to the title.

Josie posed for a steamy 16-page for the publication, all in various states of undress that would surely make Parkway Joe blush.

In one shot, the slim blonde pulls her ponytail over her head as she covers her bare breasts with her hands. In another, she poses completely nude — save for some white socks — and curls up in a chair.
