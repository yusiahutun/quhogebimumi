A Short Bio of Renee Lawless:

Born on November 30, 1960, in Knoxville, TN, Renee Lawless is an American actress and singer who is best known for her role in the famous series. "The Haves and Have Nots" as Katheryn Cryer.

Speaking of her parents, she is the daughter of  Anne Lawless and Bob Lawless. Apart from her acting skills, she also obtained Masters degree in Vocal Performance from the University of Cincinnati following her undergrad at Stetson University which shows that she is an equally accomplished singer.

She has had a lot of success in her professional life, and that has resulted in her acquiring a fair amount of name, fame, and money in her life. But she is yet to disclose any official information on her net worth along with the information about her ethnicity.
