Texas Automation Systems - Power of IIoT (Industrial Internet Of Things) | Brad Byrum

June 01, 2024

Texas Automation Systems has become a trusted automation partner by providing best-in-class automation and wireless system design, integration, programming and commissioning services.

The old adage “doing more with less” has never been truer than right now. Today companies are struggling to find and keep qualified employees as well as navigating the global supply chain crisis. While the Internet of Things (IoT) and its industrial counterpart Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), also known as Industry 4.0, have been steadily growing since their inceptions in 1999 and 2002, respectively, the current manpower and material shortages have created a perfect storm for IIoT to significantly accelerate its growth. Companies ranging from Oil & Gas, Chemicals, Water & Wastewater, Food & Beverage, Packing and Sorting to General Manufacturing are all now seeing the benefit IIoT can deliver in terms of reduced manpower requirements and improved asset utilization and protection.

Furthermore, and equally important, companies are seeing how IIoT is helping improve their Health, Safety and Environmental (HSE) and Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) scores. The complexity comes in as each company requires a unique IIoT solution to match its environment and requirements.

It becomes a difficult choice to choose between the best solution providers. There are a handful of companies that provide the latest technology packed with industry-best standards and support.

Texas Automation Systems is one of the leading names in providing unmatched and highly reliable automation and IIoT solutions. They are one of the premier names, providing the latest tech that IIoT, or industry 4.0, has to offer. It’s their prowess, command of the technology and a spectacular arsenal of solutions that make them one of the most reliable and trusted Automation solution providers.

Leading Through Automation – Texas Automation Systems (TAS)

The company began its journey in 2012, with its base of operations in Grand Prairie, Texas. Texas Automation Systems has become a trusted automation partner by providing best-in-class automation and SCADA system design, integration, programming, and commissioning services.

At its heart, Texas Automation Systems is a team of highly experienced and dedicated automation, SCADA, and telemetry professionals who are committed to making advanced technology, automation, and engineering services work for their clients. Their goal is to apply their capabilities to their client’s needs enabling them to thrive in today’s competitive landscape.

What makes TAS successful and establishes its name as one of the leading Automation solution providers?

The highly experienced and expert Texas Automation Systems team embraces the company’s Industry 4.0 philosophy and design standards. This approach and thinking allow the team at TAS to craft highly automated solutions to meet the distinctive needs of their clients.

When asked what has enable the rapid growth of Texas Automation Systems, the President, Brad Byrum, stated “I don’t believe it is one specific thing but rather our ability to consistently deliver real demonstrable value to our clients through our use of high-technology hardware and software solutions in conjunction with the high-quality services our awesome team delivers”

The team is focused on developing solutions that meet their client’s requirements and exceed their expectations. They hold numerous successful implementations with phenomenal results, earning them a dominant and respected position in the market. 

Their strong track recorded is substantiated by the following industry recognition: 

  • Rockwell Automation Silver System Integrator
  • Inductive Automation Premier System Integrator
  • Top 5 Ignition SCADA Platform System Integrator
  • Cirrus Link IIoT/MQTT Certification
  • VTSCADA Advanced Certified System Integrator

Solutions offered by TAS and what sets them apart?

Texas Automation Systems is a one-stop shop System Integrator as they provide PLC Programmers, SCADA Developers, Wireless/Network Design, VFD/Motor Control Design, Solar Power Solutions, Panel Shop, Rack/Skid Fabrication and Field Commissioning Services. They craft solutions that not only meet your needs of today but also the growth of tomorrow. So, scalability is all-inclusive when it comes to TAS and their superior solutions.  

Leadership at TAS

Texas Automation Systems has been pioneering in providing automation solutions thanks to its team of experts and indomitable service range. But what sets Texas Automation Systems apart is its core values, standards, and assurance of precision. This stems from its effervescent leadership, which holds the reins of the company’s direction and growth. The senior leadership is taking the company ahead, while mentoring the team, nurturing them to become the leaders of tomorrow, and TAS the irrefutable name in the Automation industry.

Brad Byrum is the President at TAS.

Brad has spent more than 30 years working in the industrial automation space. For the first 24 years, he worked on the supplier side applying, developing, marketing, and selling instrumentation and systems to support the automation market. 

For the past 7 years, he has worked in the system integration space creating automation solutions for a wide variety of clients. At the end of the day, Brad loves solving problems, and this is a great industry to exercise that desire.

For Texas Automation Systems, Brad is an entrepreneurial leader with a comprehensive background in leading organizations in developing sales and marketing strategies that achieve significant revenue increases, operating profit, and market position. Offering over 20 years of senior management and executive experience, earning a reputation for driving initiatives that build corporate culture imbued with high levels of motivation and ownership. 

“I consider my greatest achievements to be the success of the individuals, teams, and companies I have worked with and for. Seeing, and being part of, others growing and succeeding brings great personal satisfaction”, Brad adds.

Brad talks about the responsibilities of an entrepreneur

I believe the key responsibilities of being an effective entrepreneur are to provide the organization with a strategic vision, to build a great team, to always be looking for growth opportunities, to remove growth barriers as they arise, and to build effective processes to make the company run as smoothly and effectively as possible.

The team of TAS and what makes them unique?

The Texas Automation Systemsteam is highly focused and dedicated to meeting the high standards and reputation of the company. What allows them to strive harder and serve their clients with the best services is the way they are treated by the management and leadership of Texas Automation Systems.

Team members are listened to, respected, and encouraged to explore their ideas and carve innovation into every service they create. This, of course, includes competitive pay and benefits but also as importantly includes providing growth opportunities, welcoming their thoughts and participation in growth initiatives, recognition of a job well done, working to provide a healthy work/life balance, and having fun as often as possible.

Brad goes out of his way to direct the bulk of the company’s success to the team as a whole and specifically Jared Weakley, the Director of Automation and founding member of the company.  He adds, “we have a great team and while I may be the head of the company Jared holds all claims on being the heart of the company.” Jared’s technical leadership and commitment to getting the job done right all while truly caring about those around him is unparalleled in Brad’s opinion.
