The One Show's Alex Jones says her Strictly Come Dancing stint was a 'shambles'

March 10, 2024
blog

Alex Jones left the The One Show studio in fits of laughter as she addressed her time on Strictly Come Dancing.

The 44-year-old host was busy complimenting studio guest Katie Derham - who previously partnered with new judge Anton Du Beke - on her success in the competition in 2015.

Katie caught the TV star off-guard as she responded: "And yours too."

The Welsh star suddenly became flustered as she chirped back: "Oh not mine. Mine was a shambles."

Co-star Ronan Keating and the entire camera crew fell about the place laughing as Alex glanced down to move on with the show.

It was announced recently, that Anton - who has been a longstanding professional dancer on the programme - would take to the judging panel instead.

"What's the inside story?

"He is so talented and all the dancers respect his views and he is so knowledgeable."

She warned: "The only thing i would say is some celebrity would have the opportunity to dance with him like I did."

Back in 2011, Alex competed in the ninth series and was partnered with James Jordan.

Her time on the show wasn't a complete "shambles" as she placed fifth overall, narrowly missing out on a place in the final.
