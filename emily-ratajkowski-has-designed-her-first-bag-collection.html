Emily Ratajkowski launches bag collection with The Kooples

February 26, 2024
blog

The Kooples

Emily Ratajkowski has teamed up with The Kooples to launch a handbag collection.

The model - who is also the brand's new autumn/winter 2017 muse - has designed a style in three sizes available in 38 different variations. The classic boxy design features gold hardware and an adjustable strap. With prices between £298 and £388, the range is available to buy from 1 September.

The brand describes the collection as "a combination of savoir-faire and The Kooples' rock and roll spirit. A dual identity: strong and feminine." The collaboration marks the first time that the label have debuted bags.

Ratajkowski celebrated the news last night with a party held at Paris' Hôtel de Crillon with The Kooples' three founders, Alexandre, Laurent and Raphaël Elicha.
