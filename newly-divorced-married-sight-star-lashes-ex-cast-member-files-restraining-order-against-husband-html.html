Married at First Sight's Jaclyn Methuen reveals Ryan Ranellone broke her heart

July 25, 2024
blog

The Married at First Sight star who fell in love with her 'unattractive' groom has revealed that they have also split - making them the reality show's third and final couple to part ways after meeting for the first time at the altar six months ago.

On last night's episode of the FYI reality series' two-part reunion special, viewers were anxious to see if Jaclyn Methuen, 30, and Ryan Ranellone, 29, were still married. And while Ryan gushed about how wonderful his wife's relationship was with his mother and niece Kayla, fans soon learned that he had broken her heart just weeks after they vowed to stay married. 

'I would have made any sacrifice to be with you,' Jaclyn told Ryan. 'I would have compromised. I would have split the time in Jersey and Long Island. I would have done anything to have made this relationship work.'

When they first met at the altar, Jaclyn was unimpressed with her husband's looks but she slowly started to fall for him.

After they consummated their marriage, it was actually Ryan who started to pull away, revealing that he wasn't comfortable being physically intimate with Jaclyn because he wasn't in love with her yet. 

And according to Jaclyn, he had checked out of their relationship as soon as they stopped filming. The sales rep from Union, New Jersey, also claimed that he led her on, noting that she would have asked for a divorced on last week's season finale if he didn't tell her he was going to try and make their marriage work. 

'I am guilty of not making a tremendous effort in trying to make it work,' Ryan, who is a real estate in Long Island, admitted. 

However, when Jaclyn said that he knew she was hurting, Ryan maintained that she 'never truly expressed' how upset she really was.
