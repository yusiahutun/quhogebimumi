Charlie Sheen's daughter says upcoming boob job is going to 'save her life'

February 11, 2024

Charlie Sheen’s 19-year-old daughter has taken to TikTok to announce she’s making some major lifestyle changes, including, a boob job.

Sami Sheen, who is the daughter of TV stars Charlie Sheen and Denise Richards, revealed that she had been addicted to nicotine for five years, and now she has no choice, but to quit.

Sami Sheen says a boob job will ‘save her life’

Taking to her 95k TikTok followers, the 19-year-old revealed she had been addicted to nicotine and vaping for five years. However, had never spoken about it on social media as she likes to “keep her life private.”

Despire struggling, Sami now “absolutely has to give up” as she’s undergoing a boob job in a couple of months, and has to be “nicotine-free” for a month before.

“I’m already getting anxiety, OK? I have not gone a single day in the past five years without this m********,” the daughter of the Hollywood stars told the camera.

“I think me getting my boobs done is honestly going to save my life, because it’s forcing me to quit this,” she stated.

The 19-year-old then revealed she may even postpone her surgery because she’s “too scared to quit nicotine.”

“I’m so scared, I don’t know how I’m going to live without this thing, it’s really sad, my whole life revolves around it. I’ve depended on it for so long. My peak growing stages in life have been dependent on nicotine, which is really f****** bad,” Sami told the camera.

In the caption of the post, the daughter of the former celebrity couple penned: “please don’t be mean.”
