Meet the cast of To All the Boys: P.S. I Still Love You

Are you looking forward to watching the Netflix film?

    After a long wait, the sequel to the hugely popular is finally here! While the original Netflix film followed high schooler Lara Jean as she embarked on a fake relationship with a long-time crush, Peter, the second instalment,  will see her caught in a love triangle after a former flame, John Ambrose, comes back into her life. So who are the stars behind these beloved characters? Meet the cast of To All the Boys: P.S. I Still Love You here…

    It's fair to say that Lana has been excited for the release of the film's sequel, as she took to Instagram to post a video of the trailer on Times Square, writing: "TIMES SQUARE BABY! One more day till To All The Boys: P.S. I Still Love You premieres on @netflix @toalltheboysnetflix and I’m SHAKING."
