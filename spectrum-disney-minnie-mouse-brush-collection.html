These Minnie Mouse makeup brushes are perfect for grown-up Disney fans

June 03, 2024
blog

Minnie Mouse: our new beauty icon.

    When thinking of Disney style icons, it’s easy to think of the Disney princesses with their flawless hair and gorgeous gowns, but what about Minnie Mouse with her flirty polka dot prints and vintage hair bow? If that’s the sort of vibe with which you want to adorn your dressing table, then the Spectrum X Minnie Mouse makeup brush collection with have your (mouse) ears pricking up. Renowned for their high quality yet quirky brush sets, Spectrum’s latest launch could be one of their prettiest yet…

    The entire set will make your vanity table look palace-ready

    The collection features eight brushes, which can be bought separately ranging from £5.99 to £9.99, each in a pretty pastel shade. If you’re wanting to buy the whole set, you can choose from a sweet polka dot pouch £44.99, or camera bag (£59.99), each with the cute Minnie Mouse silhouette on and both filled with all eight brushes.

    The brushes range from big powder brushes to precise eyeliner applicators

    RELATED: For more Disney themed brush sets, you'll love Spectrum's Little Mermaid collection

    There’s also a vanity case complete with mirror, £35.99, and a sweet handheld mirror, £19.99, to really feel like a Disney princess as you apply your eyeliner. Or for the real professionals, the £44.99 belted bag, designed with make-up artists in mind, has space for your phone for those behind-the-scenes Fashion Week snaps.

    Grab the whole set by choosing one of their pretty bags

    Sophie and Hannah Pycroft, co-founders and directors of Spectrum said: “We love Minnie for her playful character, her enduring female friendships and how she encourages others to express themselves, while also constantly re-inventing her own iconic sense of style. We incorporated this into our ﬁrst Minnie Mouse Collection for Summer 18.”

    The entire collection will be available in the brand's new Carnaby Street store in Soho from 8th August, and online from 9 August - so mark the date in your calendars. Because if Disneyland is truly the most magical place on earth - why not add some magic to your everyday makeup routine?
