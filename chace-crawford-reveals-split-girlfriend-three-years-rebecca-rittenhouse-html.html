Chace Crawford has confirmed he is single after splitting from actress girlfriend Rebecca Rittenhouse.

Speaking to Us Weekly at the 2019 Tribeca Film Festival's The Boys screening in New York on Monday, the hunk admitted he's been casually dating, making no mention of Rebecca, who he had been dating for three years. 

'I've been dating around a little bit, but, you know, nothing serious,' he said.
