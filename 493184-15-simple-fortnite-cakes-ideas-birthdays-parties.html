15 simple Fortnite cakes: Best ideas for birthdays and parties

Are you a Fortnite fan looking for simple Fortnite cakes for your birthday party? Fortunately, there are numerous ideas; you will be spoilt for choice. The best and easiest ideas should go a long way in inspiring your creativity.

Fortnite is a popular online video game by Epic Games. Its popularity seems to be increasing because of the various ways people are using it in day-to-day events. For instance, simple Fortnite cakes, where characters in the games are included as design ideas.

Simple Fortnite cakes

There are several fun and creative ways to decorate a Fortnite cake. Looking at suggestions will inspire your creativity. Here are the best ideas for birthdays and parties.

1. Drip Cake with Fortnite Logo

Drip cake is an easy and quick birthday cake recipe you can make at home. You can use three layers of sponge cake to create a good base. Use white chocolate ganache or dark chocolate ganache for the drip.
