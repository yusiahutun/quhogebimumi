Mike Tyson accused of groping photographer - but insists they had sexual relationship

February 26, 2024
blog

A former photographer of boxing legend Mike Tyson has sensationally claimed he attempted to grope her breasts back in the 1980s.

Lori Grinker, 65, has released a new pictorial book, titled 'Mike Tyson', and it features snaps from Tyson's teenage years. And it's in her new book that Grinker has made the claim that Tyson attempted to grab her breasts.

"Mike could be very sweet and gentle when he was feeling it, and not very sweet or gentle when he wasn't. He once tried to grab my breast, and when I pushed him away, he got angry and threw my light meter in the snow.

"He had a somewhat spoiled side and, being the champ, grew accustomed to getting his way."

Speaking to the Daily Mail, Grinker said that Tyson never tried anything like that again - and the pair continued with a professional relationship. She also added Tyson was just an "immature teenager" at the time.

Tyson's representatives didn't deny the claim, but instead suggested Grinker and Tyson were involved in a sexual relationship at the time.

Tyson's representatives said: “In this day and age it's unfortunate that Ms. Grinker was not more careful with her words omitting the fact that she was in a sexual relationship with Mike Tyson when he was a minor with almost a decade age difference.”

His spokesperson added: “He was around 17 and had a sexual relationship with her on & off until about 21. He had sex with her also when he was champion in Atlantic City.”

Grinker has denied the claim that the pair were involved in a sexual relationship - however, if the alleged affair did occur when Tyson was 17, it would not have violated New York State consent laws enacted in 1965 despite him being a minor.
