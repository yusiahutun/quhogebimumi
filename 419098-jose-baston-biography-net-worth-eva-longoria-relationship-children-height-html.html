Early life

José Bastón (full name José Antonio Bastón Patino) was born on April 13, 1968. He was born in Mexico City, Mexico, to Ricardo Alfredo Baston Talamantes (father) and Gloria Patino (mother). Bastón is a Mexican national who boasts of Latino ethnicity.

José Bastón is a private person, and much of his personal and professional life remains hidden from the public eye. However, it is known that he grew up in Mexico (most likely Mexico City) with his family. The business mogul has a young sister, Rosalia Baston, with whom he grew up with.

Baston was born in a middle-class family. He started working as a teenager, which helped him pay for his university education at Universidad Iberoamericana (Ibero-American University).

Career

At the Universidad Iberoamericana, Baston studied Industrial Relations, which would come in handy in his media career. At the university, he met Emilio Azcárraga Milmo, the then heir – but the current owner – of Televisa Media Company – they were studying the same course.

In 1989, after graduation from university, Baston joined Televisa, one of the largest media corporations in Latin America. After that, he started working in the Marketing Department of the Tijuana area as a marketing account executive.

The rise to a media mogul

Baston was an ambitious person with a lot of tenacity. His drive for excellence saw him earn more responsibilities and promotions in Televisa over the next several years. He worked in various company departments, such as acquisitions, planning, content development, and programming. As a result, he gained a lot of experience in the running of a media business.

His performance, coupled with his close friendship with Emilio Azcárraga, helped him land an appointment as the president of Televisa International in 2008. He was responsible for content creation and the marketing of the company's content in Mexico and abroad.

In 2017, Baston resigned from his position in the company. There were a lot of speculations as he had cited personal reasons. However, it was revealed that he left the company to concentrate on his family life with his wife Eva Longoria and their children.
