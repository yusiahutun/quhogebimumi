For example, you might set a goal to increase the representation of underrepresented groups in your executive team by a certain percentage over the next year. Or you might set a target to ensure that at least 50% of all executive candidates are from diverse backgrounds.

3. Use data-driven tools

Technology can be a powerful tool for improving the Role Of Diversity and Inclusion in Executive Search. Data-driven tools can help identify potential candidates from a wider range of backgrounds and experiences, reducing the risk of relying on biases or personal networks.
