Who Are Denise Richards and Charlie Sheen's Daughters? Let's Meet Them

May 24, 2024
blog

Denise Richards and Charlie Sheen’s daughters are now teenagers making their own way in the industry. Let’s meet them.

When mentioning some of the most public romances and subsequent divorces in Hollywood, one doesn’t need to look farther than that of Charlie Sheen and Denise Richards. The duo was one of the most renowned couples in Hollywood during the early 2000s. Sheen was at the height of his career, starring in multiple back-to-back films. 

On one of those movie sets, the actor met Richards when the two starred in the 2000 movie ” Good Advice.” Still, they only started dating in 2001 after Richards guest-starred in “Spin City,” where Sheen was part of the main cast. 

Barely three months after their first date, Sheen popped the question while the pair were on vacation in Aspen. Richards said yes, and her reply kick-started what turned out to be an interesting three years for the couple. 

First, they got married in an outdoor ceremony in Brentwood, California, in front of family and friends. Then in September 2003, the couple announced they were expecting their first child.  The couple welcomed their first child, Sam Sheen, on March 9, 2004. Richards became a first-time mom while the baby was Sheen’s second. 

Just months later, the couple announced they were expecting another baby; however, Richards filed for divorce after. She gave birth to their second child, daughter Lola Rose Sheen amid their divorce proceedings. The couple ended their status as a couple in November 2006, and both went in to pursue relationships with other people. 

Denise Richards and Charlie Sheen’s Daughters 

Sami Sheen 

Sami, the couple’s first daughter, was born in 2004. The 18-year-old has followed in her parent’s footsteps by entering the entertainment industry. She has also appeared on TV with her parents multiple times. Sami starred alongside her mother on “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills during the 2009/2010 season when Richards was a prominent cast member.

After Sheen and Richards divorced, Sami first lived with her mother. However, she moved in with her dad much later and tagged residing with him as a lovely experience. 

According to multiple reports, Sami was not a fan of her mother’s rules because, like most mothers, Richards’ role was law. However, Sheen’s rules were not similar to that of his ex-wife, and Sami preferred that, so she moved in with him. 

Her decision saddened Richards, who felt she was doing everything in her power to be the best mother she could be. However, after a while, Sami returned to live with her mother once more. 

Lola Rose Sheen 

Richards and Sheen’s second child Lola was born amid the pair’s divorce proceedings. They got divorced a year after the birth of Lola. Since then, the 17-year-old has split her time between staying with Sheen and her mother.  According to a source:  

“Lola splits time between both Charlie and Denise’s homes, but Denise has been the primary caretaker for the girls for years. She’s an incredible mother who loves her kids and would do anything in the world for them.”

The youngster recently obtained her driver’s license and sometimes appeared in her parent’s or sibling’s Instagram posts. 

Sami Launched Her OnlyFans Page

In June 2022, Sami caused a stir on social media when she announced she had joined OnlyFans. The teenager shared a picture of herself donning a black bikini and encouraged fans to subscribe to her OnlyFans Page to see more. 

Sami’s post about joining the adult content-dominated platform caused many controversies as fans questioned why she would join such a platform. Meanwhile, her father, Sheen, also touched on the topic by voicing his disapproval of his daughter’s decision. In his statement, the actor noted: 

“She is 18 years old now and living with her mother. This did not occur under my roof,” he said. “I do not condone this but since I’m unable to prevent it, I urged her to keep it classy, creative and not sacrifice her integrity.”

After their divorce, Sheen has since come out to announce he is HIV positive. The actor joins the likes of Magic Johnson as stars who live with the disease. However, Richards decided to defend her daughter from the backlash in contrast to Sheen. The 51-year-old actress took to her Instagram and wrote:

“Lots of negative comments on my social this past week. I have to say, I wish I had the confidence my 18 yr old daughter has. And I also can’t be judgmental of her choices. I did Wild Things & Playboy; quite frankly, her father shouldn’t be either.”

She also praised Sami’s resolve and ability to ignore all the negative comments that followed her announcement. Richards’ disclosed it took her years to shut out harmful noise, and she sometimes still struggles to do so. 

Richards’ also defended Sami’s decision to monetize her pictures by claiming entertainers don’t get judged for being on non-monetized platforms. The actress then joked about opening an accountant herself, claiming she would love it if people paid her for her pictures.
