Color Wow One Minute Transformation

July 03, 2024
blog

What they say: Gail Federici is a perfectionist, particularly when it comes to hair products. The founder of Color Wow, and the woman who brought us the genius Root Cover Up powder, Federici confesses that the the One Minute Transformation is her favourite product. When your hair needs smoothing and slight styling adjustments, this is the cream to use.

What we say: having sworn by oil for styling, de-frizzing and smoothing for most of my life, I was sceptical about using a cream on dry hair. Two weeks and four uses later, I'm hooked.

Usability: how much you use will depend on the type and amount of hair you have. For fine hair, use roughly a five-pence-size dollop; for thicker hair, a 10-pence-size amount is best. Apply it from the mid-lengths down and comb through. If you have enough time, go over it with a hairdryer, but I have been leaving it to soak in.

Results: staying true to the promise in its name, hair looks sleeker, shinier and smoother in a minute. Having used this on clean and day-old hair, it works better on the latter, almost as a styling dry shampoo.

Insider tip: to create that beachy tousled look, tong the ends of the hair. This cream allows you to manipulate your hair and holds the style for longer.

Color Wow One Minute Transformation Styling Cream, £16.50, spacenk.com
