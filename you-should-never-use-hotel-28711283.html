Why you should never use a hotel kettle from boiling pants to chicken dinners

March 20, 2024
blog

There are plenty of unsettling things you can see in a hotel room – from bed bugs to hairs - but hotel workers have revealed that one appliance is sketchy to use; the kettle or coffee maker.

Most rooms come with one or the other so you can make a hot drink when you wake up.

But, it seems people are using the appliances for far more disgusting things than you might imagine.

Sadly, we don’t mean that holidaymakers are cooking a pot noodle or steaming their face…

Instead, they're using the kettle to clean their underwear.

On Reddit, one worker said: said: “In a past life I ran a small hotel, people doing this was literally the worst - more people do it than you’d think. Not to mention people sterilising needles, underwear all sorts of crazy sh**.”

It looks like they’re not wrong either.

One woman forgot to pack extra knickers on her holiday so she used a bizarre method to wash her dirty underwear.

Posting on Facebook, the anonymous woman said: “Staying in a hotel, that time of the month comes out of the blue. Forgot to pack knickers so came up with a good way to quickly wash them. The hotel kettle! Quick, fast and hygienic.”

People were obviously horrified.

However, she’s not the only person to use the kettle for other purposes.

A bloke previously explained that he put raw chicken in a hotel coffee machine to make dinner.

A Twitter user discovered the post and shared it on calling the idea “unhinged”.

Alexander C posted on Linkedin explaining: “I’m travelling for work and instead of eating a fancy dinner out, I’ve decided to cook a cheaper meal in the hotel room.

“Even though the hotel room didn’t have a kitchen, I managed to use the coffee machine to cook chicken with butter and garlic.

“Although my company allows me to expense dinner while travelling, I wanted to save money because I know that every dollar counts.

“It’s the little things that get you promoted.”

People did assume the move was a joke - but it's not the first time someone has used the kettle to try and cook a meal.

One tourist left Brits flabbergasted after sharing the vile meal he made in a hotel coffee maker.

Sharing a snap on Reddit, the holidaymaker revealed that he was hungry after returning to his room after a music festival.

He titled the image, “Hotel Coffee Maker Poached Beef Strips After Music Festival”.

In the image, half cooked strips of beef floated in the water inside the coffee maker’s drawer.

The bloke explained his actions on social media and said: "I was on my way home from a festival. Wanted to splurge on a nice meal but I decided to get groceries instead.

"Went to Walmart and got some thin top round, sliced against the grain and poached it."

He noted that he was usually a “good hotel guest” and did clean the machine “before and after using it”.

So, next time you go to make a brew at a hotel… consider if you’re willing to take the risk.
