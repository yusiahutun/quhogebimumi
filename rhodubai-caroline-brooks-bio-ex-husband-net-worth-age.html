Caroline Brooks has spent over a decade in Dubai. Caroline Brooks On Real Housewives Of Dubai (RHODubai)

The Real Housewives is probably the biggest reality television franchise with over 10 series in the United States while 21 internationally. And in June 2022, it added one more country to its list with its franchise The Real Housewives of Dubai.

Like always, the show featured 6 independent women, with fascinating lifestyles, all aiming to make it big in the world. Among them, Caroline Brooks on Bravo’s official page was described as someone “with an appetite for business.”

However, our star Caroline isn’t the only “Caroline” on the show. Joining her this season was her co-star Caroline Stanbury who was a fashion stylist by profession.

Also, joining the Carolines were their co-stars — Nina Ali (a co-founder of a successful cake company), Chanel Ayan (the first Black supermodel in Dubai), Dr. Sara Al Madani (a prolific public speaker), and Lesa Milan (a fashion designer).

Flaunting her “housewife passport,” Caroline took it to his IG on May 27, 2022, to write, “Pack your passport! We’re going to Dubai next Wednesday at 9/8c! #RHODubai.”

Caroline Brooks Net Worth

Caroline Brooks flaunted a net worth of under $1.5 million in 2022.

Looking back, this millionaire first started her career working at her now ex-husband’s company as their marketing manager. After garnering experience for years of what it was like to run a major business in the Emirates. She then stepped into the world of real estate and went on to become one of the first female real estate directors in the area.

Expanding her horizon, Caroline now has made a shift into the beauty sector with Glass House Salon and Spa. Her luxury salon catered to women of all colors, hair textures, and skin types. It was scheduled to officially launch on June 13, 2022.

Also, Caroline is now a reality star. She already has an IMDb page, all thanks to her time in The Real Housewives of Dubai.

Trivia: Caroline believes in giving back. She is a philanthropist who actively donated to several charity organizations

Who Is Caroline Brook’s Ex-Husband?

Caroline Brooks’ ex-husband is named Zoran. The two first met while Caroline was doing her internship in Cipriani, NYC. But at the time, Zoran was living in Dubai. So, after tying the knot the reality star had to move to Dubai with him.

Together, the duo then welcomed a son named Adam.

Unfortunately, after seven years of cherishing life as husband and wife, the duo called it quits and went their separate ways.

The details about their divorce settlement weren’t shared. However, Caroline has mentioned that she won’t hesitate to use “any connections – including her ex-husband’s – to cement her and her son’s future.”

Her son, Adam turned 10 on April 5, 2022. To celebrate his 10th birthday, Caroline rented a limo for him and his friends and took them for laser tag followed by a grand celebration.

Caroline Brooks Boyfriend

Caroline Brooks was dating her boyfriend Odion Jude Ighalo the last we checked. For those of you who don’t know, Odion was a striker for the Saudi club Al-Hilal and the Nigeria national football team. Previously, he was married to Sonia, with whom Caroline was once spotted in an IG fight.

Reportedly, Odion had invested in Caroline’s business as well.

More details on them will follow on The Real Housewives of Dubai.

Prior to Odion, Caroline dated Mombasa governor Hassan Joho, who she said treated her “like a princess.”

Overall, despite her failed relationship, Caroline isn’t afraid to love. She shared, “rejection never discourages me because I love where I am now…if you’re driven enough and know exactly what you want, you’ll get it.”

Also, she warns her now/ future boyfriend, not to cheat on her as it can be “very expensive” — “Ask my exes, “ she added.
