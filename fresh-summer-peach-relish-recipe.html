Best Fresh Summer Peach Relish Recipe

May 19, 2024
blog

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Yields: 2 c.

Prep Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 15 mins

Ingredients

Save Recipe
  • 1/4 c.

    fresh lime juice

  • 1 tsp.

    pure honey

  • 1/2

    small red onion, finely chopped

  • Kosher salt

  • 2

    ripe peaches, chopped

  • 1/2 c.

    fresh basil, thinly sliced

Directions

  • Step 1Whisk together lime juice and honey in a bowl. Add onion and season with salt. Let stand 5 minutes. Add peaches and basil and stir to combine.
