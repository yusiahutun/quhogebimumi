Katy Perry says she's taking wedding planning with Orlando Bloom slowly

October 06, 2024
blog

Katy Perry says that she's taking wedding planning slowly this time around and trying to build "an emotional foundation" before saying her vows. The singer announced her engagement to Orlando Bloom on Valentine's Day and spoke to Kiss FM this week about how the wedding planning is going.

"One step at a time," she said of her cautious approach to the nuptials, via Metro. "Definitely trying to lay the good emotional foundation for the lifetime of commitment, which is like a big deal."

Perry was famously married to British comedian Russell Brand for just over a year from 2010 to 2011. The pair enjoyed a whirlwind romance before getting engaged and having an extravagant wedding in India.

The singer also touched on how her life has changed in the past five years, adding that she won't be entering into a flashy lifestyle with her new husband.

"I’m not a very ostentatious type of person," she said. "[My world] is not crazy. It’s not like Lifestyles Of The Rich And Famous, although I’m very grateful for everything I worked hard for.’

She added: "It’s kind of representative of the last five years of how I live and the type of things I seek. I guess that would be alternative hippy methods and California hippy esoteric new-age style where you’re just trying to look inwards before going out."

Stefanie Keenan//Getty Images

In a recent interview with Capital Breakfast, Perry disclosed details of Bloom's romantic proposal.

"It was Valentine's Day, and I had to work that day," she recalled. "I just remember meeting him at dinner at this place, this Italian restaurant because, when I get a pass, I just want to just like stuff my face with pasta. So, I get there and he's clean-shaven, he's not wearing tennis shoes. I'm like, 'Ah shit, something is going down'."

When the radio host asked if Perry knew what was about to happen, she said, "Well, I mean, he was letting me... we were ordering everything, and I’m like, who is this person? He must have been so nervous—actually he was cause he was going like this [imitating heavy breathing]."

She continued: "And then I got on a helicopter, and then he asked me to marry him. And then we landed on this building, and went downstairs and my family and friends were there and like the most flowers… the most flowers you've ever seen. It's like when Kanye does that for Kim, you're like, ‘Oh my god!' And I was like, ‘Oh my god!' Anyway, it’s great, he's great. I love him very much."
