Im Packing These 12 Comfy and Practical Essentials for My Cruise

June 12, 2024
blog

I’m counting down the days until my first-ever cruise. We’ll be on board for seven days, docking in the Dominican Republic, Virgin Islands, and Puerto Rico. I’m going with my mom and sisters, so it’s all we’re talking about—so they’ve already finished up most of their shopping. I, on the other hand, have just started, and I’m going for an elevated beach look that’s versatile, comfy, and chic. 

My vacation capsule will include breezy linen pants and dresses, sandals that can be styled by the cabana or on the beach, and luxe swimwear that can double as tops. I’m a notorious over-packer, but I was able to consolidate down to 12 picks from Madewell, J.Crew, Levi’s, Birkenstock, and Coach. As a bonus, I even included my favorite Calpack packing cubes and my durable suitcase from Béis on the list.

J.Crew Capitaine Linen Shirtdress

I plan on doing two things on my trip: lounging and eating. In doing so, I need a dress that can double as a breezy cover-up over my swimsuit while also being dressy enough that I could pair it with kitten-heels—J.Crew’s Capitaine Linen Shirtdress is that dress. The linen dress comes in white, beige, black, light blue, and navy, with sizes ranging from XXS to 3XL in regular, petite, and tall. Made from 100 percent linen, the design features a button-front closure and removable belt to cinch the waist, plus pre-folded sleeves. It falls a few inches just above the knee and has an easy, relaxed fit that’ll keep you cool. 

Artfish Tank Top

I’ll likely be wearing a swimsuit for most of my trip, but my go-to uniform when I’m not in one is a pair of Levi's denim shorts and my go-to Artfish tank top. The tank comes in 39 colors from white and black to pink and green, and sizes range from XS to L. It has a close-to-the-body fit, a stretchy and thick ribbed fabric, a scooped neckline in the front and back, and a hem that stops at the hip, making it perfect for tucking. My favorite detail? The ultra-flattering thin, overlapping straps.

Birkenstock Arizona Big-Buckle Slide Sandal

I need a shoe that I don’t have to fuss with, meaning it can easily be taken on and off and be dressed up or down; these Birkenstock Arizona Big-Buckle Slide Sandals check off all those boxes. It comes in four colors, black, beige, pink, and green, with sizes ranging from 5 to 11.5. The slides have a cork-latex footbed that offers shock absorption, arch support, and a custom fit that mimics the shape of your foot with constant wear. They also feature two straps with an adjustable buckle that’s oversized and gold-plated, making it a great choice for more dressed-up occasions.

Chartou Linen Pants

Since comfort and versatility are a must on my cruise packing list, it was a no-brainer that I added a pair of linen pants from Chartou to the list. I can easily style the pants over my swimsuit or with a crisp white button-down shirt and a sweater draped over the shoulders for a preppy coastal feel. The drawstring pants come in eight colors including beige, blue, green, white, and black, and sizes XS to 2XL. They’re made from a cotton-linen blend and feature a high-rise fit, two front pockets, and a full-length relaxed design.
