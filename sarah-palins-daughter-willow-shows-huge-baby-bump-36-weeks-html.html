Sarah Palin's daughter Willow shows off her HUGE baby bump at 36 weeks

March 03, 2024
blog

Willow Palin is proudly showing off her enormous baby bump in a new Instagram snap and video.    

'So thankful to be able to carry our girls this far,' Willow, 25, wrote in the caption accompanying her Saturday post. '36 weeks and counting!' 

Willow can be seen wearing a grey sports bra - flashing a hint of a tattoo on her ribs - black leggings, sandals and socks as she showcases her protruding belly.

In the short video, Willow can be seen smiling as she turns left and right in a mirror while rubbing her belly. 

Willow has been chronicling her growing baby bump on Instagram since announcing she and husband, Rickey Bailey, were expecting twins in May. 

On Monday, Willow - who is due in December - took to Instagram Stories to share a photo of herself sitting on the floor in her babies' bedroom with her 'Friends' long-sleeve shirt hiked up over her bare stomach. 

'Countdown to babies,' she captioned the image, which shows her surrounded by piles of baby clothes that she had folded, while getting her girls' nursery ready for their arrival. 

The mom-to-be has on comfortable purple sweatpants, and her dark hair is pulled up into a high ponytail. Her little dog is sitting next to her as she looks at all the baby clothes she still has to put away. 

The picture was likely taken by Willow's 18-year-old sister, Piper Palin, who posted a video of the mom-to-be's baby bump on her own Instagram Story that same day. 

Willow also shared a snapshot of all the baby gifts her older sister, Bristol Palin, had sent her, writing: 'Presents for the babes and post partum goodies for me from @bsmp2.' 

Bristol, who lives in the suburbs of Austin, Texas, missed Willow's baby shower that took place in Alaska in September, instead opting to get baptized with her son, Tripp, that day.
