20 short morning prayers for friends to bless their day

April 14, 2024
blog

Prayer is the primary way Christians speak to God. You can talk to God on behalf of your friends to bless their day. Morning prayers for friends go a long way to ensure they have a stress-free day. In addition, they show how much you are connected, and how you bear one another's burdens.

Praying for a friend before the day starts is a sure way that God's Presence will be evident in their life throughout the day. It's a sure way of trusting in His power and relying on Him fully to make your friend's day successful.

Prayer draws believers closer to God and shows total dependence on Him. You can pray for friends who are near or far away. If you cannot hug your friends with your hands, embrace them with prayers wherever they are every morning.

What are the prayer items when you are praying for your friends? All your friends have needs such as good health, financial breakthrough, stability in marriage, success in education and many others. Always commit your friend's needs to God to bless their day.

Prayer of protection

The world is full of many disasters such as accidents, diseases, and attacks. It is essential to ask God to protect your friends against all these as they go through their duties every morning. A simple message such as have a blessed day my friend can make a significant difference. The following are sample prayers for your friend’s protection.

Father, we worship You. Thank you for giving us a new day and helping us to know your word. As we start this day, I commit all my friends to you. I prohibit all forces of the evil one in their lives. The Bible says whatever we refuse on earth will be forbidden in heaven, and whatever we permit on earth shall be allowed in heaven. Dear God, we let your Holy Spirit get into their ways to guard them. In Jesus name, we pray, Amen.
