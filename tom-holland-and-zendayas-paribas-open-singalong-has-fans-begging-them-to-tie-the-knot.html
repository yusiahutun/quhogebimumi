Tom Holland and Zendaya's Paribas Open singalong has fans begging them to tie the knot

Tom Holland and Zendaya have given us the cutest content, as they made a rare public appearance as a couple, in the crowd of the BNP Paribas Open.

The celebrity couple confirmed their relationship in 2021, and have been pretty low-key ever since. Of course, we get to see Tom Holland gushing over Zendaya via Instagram, but nothing beats a cute video of everyone’s fave couple. Now, they’re indulging in some ‘public karaoke’ together, and the internet is obsessed.

Tom Holland and Zendaya sing along to Whitney Houston at BNP Paribas Open

The Hollywood couple had their tennis shoes on from the stands as they enjoyed a ‘date day’ watching the Women’s Final in Indian Wells Tennis Garden in California.

They got into the spirit during the entertainment between sets, as they were adorably filmed singing along to Whitney Houston’s I Wanna Dance with Somebody.

Tom looked particularly into the moment as he could be seen throwing his hands up to the beat, as Zendaya beamed and looked on, while singing along.

Once again, the Euphoria actress did not disappoint with her fashion, as she stunned in a cream tennis skirt and crop top, with a cute jacket on top.

The Spider-Man actor also looked the part, wearing a smart, yet stylish, brown button-down shirt over a white T-Shirt.

The sporty outing comes just before Zendaya is set to star as a tennis coach in the upcoming Challengers movie.

In another adorable clip, Holland and Zendaya could be seen ‘goofing around’ clapping and cheering together, as they co-ordinated their hand movements.
