Easy shifting methods

What is the best shifting method? There are various shifting methods available. They range from extremely simple to somewhat more complex. You can live the life you want by shifting your consciousness from your current reality to an ideal one, a concept known as reality turning. It entails sending your consciousness to the world you want to live in through techniques such as self-hypnosis, meditation, visualisation, and affirmations.

If you are fascinated by the shifting concept, these reality-shifting methods listed below might appeal to you.

1. 5 Senses technique

The 5 Senses technique is among the easy shifting methods. Lie in a comfortable position with your entire body relaxed. Begin counting up from one pausing at intervals to recite affirmations. Visualise your desired reality (DR) while using all of your senses. When you reach five, open your eyes, focus on entering your DR or fall asleep in your current reality (CR) and wake up in your DR.

2. Alarm clock shifting method

How do you turn realities using the alarm clock technique? It is pretty straightforward if you have an alarm clock. There are two different techniques you can use for this method. At first, you visualise your desired reality self setting an alarm clock. Affirm to yourself that your alarm will go off and wake you in the desired reality.

The second method involves setting an alarm clock in your current reality (CR). Go to sleep, and let the alarm wake you up. Then, drift off again, but do your best to slip into a lucid dream state.

3. Cinns shifting method

If you're wondering how to change your reality, the Cinns technique is a great place to start. Turn on your Subliminal stimuli once you're in a relaxed state. Clear your mind and begin counting from 1 to 100 while inhaling and exhaling. Stop every ten numbers and say affirmations about your desired reality (DR). When you reach 100, repeat your affirmations for as long as necessary before opening your eyes in your DR.

4. Coraline shifting

The Coraline technique involves crawling through a tunnel into another world. Lie down, eyes closed, visualising your current reality. Visualise yourself getting up, using a key to open a door with a tunnel. Crawl through the tunnel to your desired reality (DR).

5. Hogwarts shifting method

If you're looking for popular reality-shifting methods, the Hogwarts technique is ideal. Close your eyes and imagine yourself approaching a magic mirror with this technique. Step through the mirror and into Hogwarts.

The second Hogwarts shifting technique is a variation of the Pillow method. At bedtime, place your Hogwarts shifting script under your pillow and close your eyes, visualising yourself at Hogwarts. I drift off and wake up at Hogwarts.

The third method is a variant of the Raven method. Put on your Subliminals, lie down, close your eyes, and begin counting from 100 to one. Recite affirmations at regular intervals and imagine yourself at Hogwarts. Allow yourself to drift off, then wake up in Hogwarts.

6. Hug shifting

If you are looking for shifting methods for beginners, then the hug shift technique is one of them. To turn realities using this method, close your eyes and imagine yourself awakening in your DR. In your mind, get out of bed, place your feet on the floor, and imagine someone from your DR entering the room. Spend time with them before saying good-by and repeating affirmations to yourself until you fall asleep.

7. In your arms method

In this method, you visualise a particular person in your DR. Lie down with a pillow under you and rest your head, pretending to be that person. With your eyes closed, try to convince yourself as vividly as possible that it is that person and you are in your DR cuddling them. Say affirmations and then count down from 100 to one. When ready, open your eyes in your DR or fall asleep and awake there.

8. Lucid dream-shifting method

There are several different lucid dreaming techniques. They include the Lucid dreaming portal method, the Lucid dreaming TV screen and the desired reality scenario Lucid dream. In the portal approach, you fall asleep, look for or create an outlet in your imagination and step through it to enter your DR.

The second approach, the Lucid dreaming TV screen method, entails using a TV, computer screen or book as your dream portal. The third method is to create your DR scenario using creativity. While dreaming, deliberately try to make your ideal DR scenario.

9. The ADHD method

The ADHD method requires headphones for ideal results. Lie comfortably, listening to 8D soundtracks and choose the appropriate affirmations. Then try and sync your breath with the audio, counting from 1 to 5 after each declaration. Repeat the process, and after a while, you'll begin shifting. Finally, visualise the reality you wish for, and you'll wake up there.

10. The Alice In Wonderland method

Alice in Wonderland is a fictional book about a girl and her adventures in a magical wonderland. The Alice in Wonderland technique involves manifesting your reality by role-playing Alice in the magical world.

First, lie in a comfortable position and envision yourself under a tree. A person from your desired reality will approach you, directing that you follow them down a rabbit hole. Let go of all memories from your current reality and focus on your desired one. You will see a locked door and unlock it to enter your dream reality.

11. The Elevator method

The elevator method is among the most used reality-shifting methods for beginners. Take an imaginary elevator to your DR. Place yourself in a comfortable position and imagine yourself entering an elevator. Imagine different worlds as you ride the elevator up, passing through each floor. Exit the elevator when you have climbed to the appropriate level to enter your desired reality. It might help to imagine your energy levels increase as the elevator rises.

12. The Eleven method

This method alters reality by taking lessons from Eleven from the television show Stranger Things. Lay down and cover your eyes with a fabric or a bandana to block out the light. 13. The Estelle method

The Estelle technique entails dancing to your favourite happy music with your desired partner. Imagine the two of you filled with joy and euphoria as you enjoy each other's company. 14. The Eye method

The eye technique involves moving your eyes back and forth. To begin, lie down on your bed and close your eyes and count to 100. Keep your DR in mind and what you want to see as you count. After you've reached 100, keep reciting affirmations to yourself while moving your eyes back and forth. When you're ready, open your eyes and enter your desired reality.

The falling method allows you to enter your desired reality through a doorway. Consider yourself in a long corridor lined with doors. Open the door at the end of the hall while imagining your DR. The Julia method requires you to begin by writing a script, followed by a few minutes of meditation. Following that, recite affirmations related to your DR. The mirror technique is one of the best shifting methods available. To use the mirror method, lie down and imagine yourself standing in front of a mirror. In the mirror, you should see hints of your desired reality. As you notice your desires, place your hand on the mirror and use it as a portal to your desired existence. 20. The Piano method

Did you know that imagining yourself playing the piano can help you shift? Put yourself in a comfortable position and imagine yourself seated at a piano in a crowded room. Begin playing the piano and try to picture the sounds as much as possible.

When the song finishes, exit the chamber and proceed down a corridor lined with doors, then through the door with a bright white light. Recite affirmations to yourself as you walk through the doors to your DR. After the light fades, open your eyes to enter your DR.

21. The Pillow technique

The pillow technique is one of the most popular shifting methods without visualisation. With the pillow-shifting method, write your affirmations on paper and go over them before bed. After you've finished reading those sentences, put the list beneath your pillow and go to sleep.

When you wake up, you'll be in your desired reality. Include all of your senses when composing a script for your DR, including touch, feeling, smell, sight, and taste.

The Raven technique, one of the most popular shifting methods, asks you to lie in a quiet environment in a starfish configuration. Then, begin counting from one to one hundred, breathing in and out, and repeating particular shifting affirmations while maintaining your focus on your DR. When you reach 1, focus on entering and staying in your dream reality for a time to transform your reality.

23. The Sunni method

To begin the Sunni technique, lie comfortably and relax your body and mind. Then, start envisioning your DR in every aspect. While picturing anything, use all five of your senses. Secondly, concentrate on reciting the needed affirmations until you see a change in your symptoms. If your practice was a success, you should be able to see your DR at the end. To apply the Sunni technique, you must have a powerful imagination.

24. The Train method

Imagine yourself travelling to the desired reality on a train. Close your eyes while sitting comfortably and inhale deeply to unwind. Think of yourself as a passenger on a train travelling to the realm you wish. Just open your eyes when you sense that the train has stopped, and keep telling yourself positive things as you do so.

25. Valentino shifting method

The Valentino strategy is an excellent technique to adopt if you want to move to your desired reality because you are looking forward to spending time with someone special there. You must first write a letter to that person, followed by a script. Then, before shutting your eyes, say positive affirmations. Finally, envision a room and open your eyes when you are ready for your DR, or you can fall asleep and wake up in your preferred state.
