About

Anthony Anderson is a 53 year old American Actor. Born Anthony Alexandre Anderson on 15th August, 1970 in Los Angeles, California, United States, he is famous for Law and Order, Black-ish, All About The Andersons.. His zodiac sign is Leo.

Tracee Ellis Ross is a 51 year old American Actress. Born Tracee Joy Silberstein on 29th October, 1972 in Los Angeles, California, USA, she is famous for her roles as Joan Clayton in the UPN/CW sitcom Girlfriends, Dr. Rainbow “Bow” Johnson in the ABC sitcom Black-ish, its Freeform spin-off sitcom series Grown-ish, and its ABC single-camera spin-off sitcom series Mixed-ish, Dr. Carla Reed in the BET original family sitcom Reed Between the Lines, Kristin in the 2009 romantic comedy film Labor Pains, Gloria Parkes in the CBS procedural forensics crime drama series CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Grace Davis in the 2020 musical comedy-drama film The High Note, Ellen in the ABC medical drama series Private Practice, and Cynthia in the 2007 romantic comedy-drama film Daddy’s Little Girls.
