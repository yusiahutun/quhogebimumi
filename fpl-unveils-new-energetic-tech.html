FPL Unveils New Energetic Tech

blog

Florida Power & Light Company (FPL) recently unveiled a free resource to help families increase their homes’ energy efficiency and make more empowered energy choices. FPL’s House of Savings is an innovative, interactive tool that uses augmented reality to help customers learn how to save on their monthly energy bill. Customers can enter the House of Savings here.

In the House of Savings, users move through a virtual home and answer energy-related questions by interacting with different appliances in the home: the kitchen, office, and laundry room. Throughout the experience, participants learn which appliances have the biggest impact on their bills and see how simple changes can add up to savings. Mobile phones or tablets with a camera are required. 

Customers will learn how to apply better energy-saving habits throughout their own home. If they answer all the questions correctly, customers will learn how to save more than $30 on their monthly energy bill.   

The House of Savings also features the free FPL Energy Manager, an all-in-one tool that allows customers to view the what, where, and why of their home’s energy use. Customers can access the FPL Energy Manager by logging into their account. 

The House of Savings will be available year-round. As temperatures drop in the coming months, the home will feature refreshed tips for the winter season.
