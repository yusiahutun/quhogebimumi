NF Announces The Second Leg Of His North American Tour

April 23, 2024
blog

(CelebrityAccess) — Hot on the heels of his successful European tour, rapper NF announced the second leg of his North American “The Search” tour will take place in Spring 2020.

The 28-date run kicks off on April 9th at Verizon Arena in Little Rock, Ark and concludes on May 18th at Park in the Park at Petco Park in San Diego, CA.

Other key dates include the Hammerstein Ballroom in New York City; the Byline Bank Aragon Ballroom in Chicago; REBEL in Toronto; and the Bill Graham Civic Auditorium in SF.

NF has been tearing up the charts with Searcher, which is his second consecutive album to debut at #1 on the Billboard Top 200.

Since it launched in August, “The Search” has shifted 130,000 equivalent album units and racked up more than 120 million global streams to date.
