Jennifer Lawrence gives birth to first child with art dealer husband Cooke Maroney

January 26, 2024

Jennifer Lawrence has reportedly given birth to her first child with husband Cooke Maroney.

The Academy Award winning actress welcomed her baby in Los Angeles county, reported TMZ who obtained public records.

The gender of the baby and the exact date the 31-year-old gave birth is currently not yet known.

JLaw first caused speculation when she was spotted with a baby bump over the summer while out in New York City.

The exciting news of The Hunger Games star's pregnancy was later confirmed by a representative to PEOPLE.

The blonde beauty later posed alongside her co-star Leonardo DiCaprio in December as she showcased her baby bump on the red carpet at the Don't Look Up premier.
