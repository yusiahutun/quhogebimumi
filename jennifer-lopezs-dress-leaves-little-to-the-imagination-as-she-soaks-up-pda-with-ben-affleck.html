Jennifer Lopez's dress leaves little to the imagination as she soaks up PDA with Ben Affleck

July 23, 2024
blog

The IT couple, JLo and Ben Affleck, take their PDA to the Red Carpet yet again, this time at ELLE’s Women in Hollywood gala. Jennifer Lopez left little to the imagination in a stunning dress ensemble paired with a chrome breastplate.

The celebrity couple took photos on the Red Carpet together, even putting on a public display of affection, after the Hollywood star whispered something in his wife, Jennifer Lopez‘s, ear.

Jennifer Lopez’s dress steals the show as she poses with Ben Affleck

The On The Floor singer opted for some futuristic fashion, wearing a chrome breastplate. As well as showing off some underboob, the plate also showed off the 54-year-old’s toned midriff.

She paired the ‘out-there’ top with a simple black pencil skirt and some black high-heeled pumps.

To accessorize, the music star wore silver earrings, which rested below her shoulder. She slicked her hair back into a neat bun, to show off the outfit in all its glory.

The Gone Girl star opted for a classic black suit and tie combo.

Ben and JLo seemed in high spirits, laughing and joking together as they posed for pics. Affleck could be seen whispering into his partner’s ear before the two shared a cute kiss.

The ELLE event is an annual event that celebrates entertainment and was attended by a range of A-List celebrities including Kate Beckinsale and Kate Winslet.

The star felt ‘so honored’ to be at the event

As well as posing for pics, the star also gave a speech at the prestigious event, in which she said she was “honored” to be in attendance.

“Oh, my God. What a night. I’m gonna go off script, but I’m gonna come back to this. I feel so honored to be here. It is like a dream,” she said (via Elle).

She then went on to say how “moving” it was to be “in a room full of powerful women who are championing each other and who are caring about one another and who are uplifting each other.”

JLo was gifted ‘icon’ status at the award, announcing that it was her fifth award with the title. “Of all the things that I thought I would grow up to be when I was a little girl, the last thing you know that was kind of on my list was ICON,” she joked.
