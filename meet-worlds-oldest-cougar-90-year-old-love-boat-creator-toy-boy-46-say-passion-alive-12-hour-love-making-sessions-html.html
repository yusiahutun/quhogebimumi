A 90-year-old woman and her 46-year-old toyboy, a former bodybuilder, have opened up about their passionate relationship.

In last night's episode of TLC's Extreme Cougar Wives, Jeraldine Saunders, the creator of Seventies TV series The Love Boat, gushes about having been in a relationship with former Mr California, Donaldo Monroy, for '11 beautiful years'.

And despite their 44-year age gap, Mr Monroy insists their romance is still going strong. 'Redheads are the best in bed,' he says. 'One time we started making love from 12 noon to 12 midnight without stopping.'
